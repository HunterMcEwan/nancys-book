A hand-drawn pedigree sketch in blue ink. Reading from top to bottom:

> **John Stoney — Elizabeth Porcher Gaillard**

Their children, on the next line:

> Christopher FitzSimons — **Elizabeth Porcher Stoney**
>
> **Edgar George Stoney** — Anne O. Bryan

The next generation below:

> Catherine FitzSimons — Dr. Robert Withers Vaux
>
> Eliza C. Stoney

Issue of Catherine FitzSimons and Dr. Robert Withers Vaux, at the bottom of the chart, bracketed together as siblings:

> Robert Vaux
>
> Robbie } Vaux
> Eddie  }
> Minnie — married Whitford.

Marginal notes in the same hand:

Top left, in pencil:

> Sam did this on the back of your letter, I have only copied it. H.S.

Lower left:

> Mary Vaux Whitford is Mrs. Harold Brecht
> 5313 Bayton St.
> Germantown, Pa.
> Cleland Vaux is dead.
