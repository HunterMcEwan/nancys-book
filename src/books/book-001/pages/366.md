A composite album page laying out a small genealogical record of the Samuel Gaillard FitzSimons Jr. branch in photographs and clippings.

**Top of page — handwritten genealogical key (pencil, with red and blue ink corrections):**

> Samuel Gaillard Fitz Simons 2nd. B. Sept 24th 1904
>   — M — in Petersburg, Va. Jan 24th 1931
> Mary Hadlock — B-Aug 11th 1911 D-Aug 24th 1958
>
> 1) Samuel Gaillard Fitz Simons 3rd. B. 1933
> 2) Mary Ann Fitz Simons. B. 1935  M Clarence Edwin McLane. April 27th 1957
> 3) Grace B. Dec. 24th 1938
>                                              Dunkin McLane. B. Dec. 1st 1959

*[The "2nd" on the key block is Samuel Gaillard FitzSimons Jr. — the compiler Amy's brother (Bub/Buck/Sambo). The "3rd" is his son SGFS III, who married Jeanne Louise Newhall in 1957 (see invitation below) and whose own son SGFS IV was born Dec. 24, 1961 — see page 367.]*

**Photographs (clockwise from upper left):**

A studio portrait of a young woman with dark hair and a soft smile, three-quarter view. Pencil caption beneath:

> Mary H. F. S.

*[Mary Hadlock FitzSimons, wife of Samuel Gaillard Jr.]*

A small colour snapshot of a smiling toddler in a striped shirt seated on a step:

> Dunkin McLane

*[Son of Mary Ann FitzSimons and Clarence Edwin McLane, b. Dec. 1, 1959.]*

A sepia-toned snapshot of a baby in a christening gown:

> Sam "B"

*[Baby photo of Samuel Gaillard FitzSimons Jr. ("Sambo / Bub / Buck"), b. 1904.]*

A small black-and-white head-and-shoulders studio photograph of a young man in jacket and tie:

> Sam F. S. Jr. 1956

*[Samuel Gaillard FitzSimons III, son of SGFS Jr. and Mary Hadlock; b. 1933.]*

A larger studio portrait of a young woman in a Peter-Pan-collared blouse, smiling:

> Mary Ann F. S.

*[Mary Ann FitzSimons, who married Clarence E. McLane April 27, 1957.]*

A larger black-and-white school-portrait of a girl with pearl necklace:

> Grace F. S.   1957

*[Grace FitzSimons, b. Dec. 24, 1938 — see also the Andrew Jackson Senior Class clipping below where she is named class treasurer.]*

**Newspaper clipping (left centre):**

> **Miss FitzSimons**
> **Weds Navy Man**
>
> The marriage of Miss Mary Ann FitzSimons and Clarence E. McLane, USN, was solemnized April 27 at 7 p.m. at the Holy Cross Episcopal Church, with the Rev. Charles L. Keyser officiating.
>
> The bride is the daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Samuel G. FitzSimons of this city, and the bridegroom is the son of Mrs. C. C. Harston of Orlando.
>
> Mrs. McLane is a graduate of Andrew Jackson High School. The bridegroom is presently stationed at Cecil Field Naval Air Station.

> **ANDREW JACKSON SENIOR CLASS OFFICERS**
