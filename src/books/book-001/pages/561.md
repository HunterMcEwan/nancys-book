> [Portrait of] FRANK L. FITZSIMONS
>
> # VFW Honors FitzSimons, Elects Ray
>
> Charles Lenoir Ray, senior vice-commander of Hedrick-Rhodes Post 5206, was elected post commander last night at a VFW membership meeting.
>
> In a special ceremony, Frank W. FitzSimons was cited as "Man Of The Year."
>
> Ray succeeds Grady T. Carland as post commander.
>
> Other officers elected are Arch de Castrique, senior vice-commander; Manuel Torres, junior vice-commander; Charles Worley, quartermaster; Aiken Pace, post surgeon; Rev. Major Edwards, chaplain; Frank Coiner, judge advocate; and Edward Mottsman.
>
> (Continued on page four)

*[The "Frank W. FitzSimons" of the article is almost certainly Frank L. FitzSimons Sr. of Hendersonville; the same honor and post are described elsewhere in the scrapbook under the correct initials.]*
