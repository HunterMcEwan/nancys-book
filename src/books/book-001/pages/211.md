A handwritten letter sheet in cursive ink. The page is numbered **3** at top center.

> much more accessible stage
> than when you were here.
> He is an interesting child, &
> wins my heart by looking
> like you, & Mother, & Katie —
> with a lovely complexion. —
> He has a little ripple in his
> laugh like Kate's too — Dear
> [Macinie] I think of all you
> were to her & us last year,
> and what a difference your
> coming, and all you did
> for her, made to her & to us.
> We often have to do for others,
> what has been done for us,
> but not often to the ones
> who loved & cared for us — but
> you did for her all the tender
> loving help she needed — as she
> did for you in your Babyhood.
> I know you feel it as we
> do, to have been a treasure
