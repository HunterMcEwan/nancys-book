## Top — closing of clipped article (heading cut off)

> SEARCH FOR LOST PILOT
> FAILS TO BRING RESULT
>
> [Top of article cut off; visible closing paragraph:]
>
> The officers of the submarine were Lt. Comdr. Charles H. Jones, J. C. Foley, Lieut. J. M. Smith, Lieutenants.
>
> Clarken, Pa., Oct. 7. (By A. P.)— Issuance of searchers, including ten airplanes, National Guardsmen, hunters and a dozen airmen, who scoured a wide area of the territory north until early today in the search of Charles H. Jones, missing aircraft pilot, returned today exhausted with-out having found any trace of the airman. Smith was nowhere to be seen of nothing over this entire air days ago.
>
> Convinced that Jones plane fell somewhere in the district east of this city, Capt. Carl F. Egge of the Air mail service announced this morning that even the missing pilot's fate is determined.
