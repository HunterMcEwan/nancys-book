**1**

> John Stoney b.c. 1745, d. Oct. 19, 1821, m. Elizabeth Caulfield. They came to Charlestown from Ireland in 1774. She died Nov. 8, 1820 aged 71. They lived about Beaufort, finally on Hilton Head Island. He died as a result of a hunting accident.

**2.** **James** b. Nov. 30, 1772 d. Feb. 12, 1837.

> m. Elizabeth, daur. of Dr. George Moss or Morse. *(The Gaillard & Du Pré family record from Tarrer.)*

> Eliza b. Mar. 27, 1784  d. before 1790.

**3.** **John Stoney**, b. July 18, 1780, d. Nov. 6, 1839

> m. 1st, Harriet Elgin, dau. of Edgar Welsh *(April 4, 1801)*  She died June 9, 1809. Two sons Edgar and George predeceased their mother apparently.

> m. 2nd Nov. 28, 1805 Elizabeth Porcher, daur. of Capt. Peter [Gaillard] of the Rocks, b. Jan. 8, 1789, d. July 28, 1856.

**3·1.** Elizabeth Porcher Stoney — married Christopher Fitz Simons

**3·2.** John Safford Stoney  died unm.

**3·3.** Peter Gaillard Stoney (1808–1881) m. 1835, Anna Maria, dau. Thomas Porcher of Ophir.

**4.** Edgar George Stoney ( 1812? ) d. 1838

**5.** Christopher Fitz Simons Stoney ( 1816? ) [unm.]

**6.** Samuel David Stoney (1822–1889) m. 1832, Harriet Porcher, dau. Peter Gaillard of Hagdon Hill.

**7.** Eugene Lydia Stoney married Herbert A. Watherson

**8.** Theodore Stoney m. Rosa, dau. Geo. Mills

**9, 10, 11.** *infants* William, Henry and James
