## Letter — Columbia, October 22nd, from Susan M. FitzSimons to Mr. (Thomas E.) Osborne

> Columbia
> Oct 22*nd*
>
> Dear Mr. Osborne
>
> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I was very glad
> to hear of that little
> girl and would like
> to know what you
> have named her? —
> I am glad also to
> tell you that Gaillie
> is fast getting strong
> he has gained flesh
> rapidly since we got
> to Columbia & has
> a fine appetite for
> all his meals but
> still eats no meat
> at all. His limbs
> were very stiff from
> lying nearly three
> months in bed —
> & he still goes up &
> down stairs with
