> **Donald Allston Jr. Receives C. & S. Bank Promotion**
>
> Donald Allston Jr. has been promoted to assistant trust officer of the Citizens and Southern National Bank of South Carolina.
>
> Mr. Allston is a native of Yonge's Island. He is a graduate of The Citadel and the University of South Carolina Law School and served in the Navy. He became associated with the bank in 1951.
>
> His wife is the former Miss Marguerita FitzSimons of Hendersonville, N.C. They have two children.
