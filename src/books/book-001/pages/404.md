An engraved invitation, centered on a cream card in copperplate script.

> Mr. and Mrs. Albion Williamson Knight
>
> request the honour of your presence
>
> at the marriage of their daughter
>
> Ann Seymour
>
> to
>
> Mr. James Pickens Walker, Jr.
>
> on Thursday, the twenty-sixth of June     **1941**
>
> at eight o'clock in the evening
>
> The Church of the Good Shepherd
>
> Jacksonville, Florida

*[The year "1941" is added in blue ink at the right margin in a later hand.]*
