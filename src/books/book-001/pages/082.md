A largely blank sheet showing the outer panel of a folded letter. The address is written in brown ink across the centre, with a small red wax seal at the lower edge of the sheet. A pencilled annotation in a later hand is squeezed beneath the addressee's name and is initialled at right.

> Mr. Samuel G. Barker
>
> *[pencilled, beneath, in a later hand]:* my great gra\[nd]father — *A.F.W.*
>
> Charleston S.C.
