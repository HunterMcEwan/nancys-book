F-S.'s grandmother Maria Anne Hadley. Includes a note that 'on the blank page in the beginning of [book] is written: \"The Altar bloomed June 14th, 1846 in Broad Street near Friend Street\"' — apparently the address of the Hadley house. Records two marriages: John L. Hadley to Sarah J. Bryant, Nov. 9, 1826; and Benjamin J. Whaley to Sarah Jane Hadley, May 10th, 1849. A closing entry identifies John Lucius Hadley as the son of the Rev. Dr. John Hadley, D.D., born in Newick Town near London, England, May 26, 1802. A pencilled footnote in a later hand records that 'this house and surroundings are still owned by J. B. Bryant — on Broad St.' signed 'Minnie Perry FitzSimons 1924.' Ink is faded and the handwriting is closely spaced; several readings are uncertain."
