> Married —
>
> **John L. Hedley** to **Sarah J. Bryant** Nov. 9th 1826.
>
> Married —
>
> **Benjamin J. Whaley** to **Sarah Jane Hedley** May 10th 1849.
>
> **John Lucius Hedley**, the son of the Rev.d Dr. **John Hedley, D.D.** was born in **Newick Town near London, England**. May 26, 1802.

*[The Hedleys are the compiler's mother's maternal line. **Maria Ann Hedley** (sister of John Lucius) married **Mr. Mauger** and then **Edward Perry**, becoming the grandmother of Minnie Perry FitzSimons and the great-grandmother of the compiler Amy. The transcriber "Minnie P. FitzSimons" is Mary Anne Perry FitzSimons, the compiler's mother, working from the original Hedley family Bible in 1924, ten years before her death.]*
