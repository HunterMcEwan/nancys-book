The third sheet of the letter, in blue ballpoint, continuing without break from the previous page.

> day of my life. Eventually, I'm sure I'll go to some sort of graduate school, but am content at the moment to enjoy my leisurely evenings.
>
> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;NCNB (No. Carolina Nat'l Bank) has made an offer to buy Pan American. If all is approved, the takeover will occur Jan 1, 1986. At present, we do not know what effect it will have on us, but it could certainly mean some drastic changes. It's all a wait and see at the moment.
