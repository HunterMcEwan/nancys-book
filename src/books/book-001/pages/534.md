> **Here is Called 'Charleston's Cyclopedia'**

*[Editorial note: This page functions as the family's memorial scrapbook spread for Ellen M. FitzSimons. The clippings span her 1952 retirement and her 1955 death and funeral, and were evidently pasted in by the compiler (Amy FitzSimons, Mrs. James Pickens Walker) after Ellen's death. The pages 528–533 immediately preceding this spread are correspondence to and about Ellen — letters from her nephew Frank L. FitzSimons, from a niece Ellen, from the College of Charleston offering her an honorary degree, and from a Hendersonville uncle — all preserved as part of the same memorial gathering.]*
