Anna FitzSimons married Willie Gaillard of Eutawville, S. C.

Her sister Lizzie FitzSimons married Willie Ravenel. They were 1st cousins of my father's generation.

Etta Gaillard, who was a sister of Willie Gaillard married my father's brother Seaman FitzSimons, who was Anna & Lizzie's father.
