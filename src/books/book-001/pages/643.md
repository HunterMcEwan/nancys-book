The body is a typed extract from the Parish Register burial record for Rev. B. F. Dunkin Perry (1874), followed by a sworn statement of Harry E. Lawhon Jr., Rector of Grace Episcopal Church, that the excerpt is identical with that found in the said Parish Register on page 428, sworn before a Notary Public for South Carolina on the thirteenth day of June, 1969. Two signatures in blue ink and a partial notary seal at the lower left. Pages 643 and 644 are duplicate scans of the same document."

*[Written in blue ink at the top of the page in the compiler's hand:]*

> Grace Church
> Camden, South Carolina

*[Typed extract:]*

> January 15th 1874. Rev. B. F. D. Perry. Rector of the church, was buried in the Cemetary. Officiating clergyman Rev. John Johnson

*[Typed certification:]*

> State of South Carolina
> County of Kershaw
>
> Personally appeared before me Harry E. Lawhon, Jr., Who, being sworn deposes and says that he is Rector of Grace Episcopal Church, Camden, South Carolina, and is custodian of the Parish Register of Said Church from which the foregoing excerpt has been taken and that said excerpt is identical with that found in the said Parish Register on page 428, being part of the records of Burials for the year 1874.

*[Signed:]*

> Harry E. Lawhon, Jr., Rector

*[Below:]*

> Sworn to before me this thirteenth day of June, 1969.

*[Notary's signature, partly illegible:]*

> [Vivian M. Taylor]
> Notary Public for South Carolina
