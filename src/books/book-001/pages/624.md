A large fold-out genealogical chart of the Perry family, headed **PERRY** at the upper left. The chart begins at the top with the earliest **Edward Perry** of the family and traces descendants for several generations downward, bracketing in by marriage the Bowles, Tilden, Drayton, Mauger, Bessellieu, Howell, and FitzSimons lines. The latest generation along the lower edge of the sheet includes **Maria Ann Perry**, **Benjamin Faneuil Dunkin Perry**, **Julia Virginia Bessellieu**, **Edward Jackson Fifty\[?]**, and others, with their parents bracketed above them.

A long handwritten note in the upper-right corner of the chart appears to be a covering message from the cousin who compiled the tree (compare the letter on pages 626–627), discussing her progress in tracing the **Charleston** and **Saint Pauls Parish** Perry lines and asking for additional family information.

*[The fold-out is closely written in pencil and light blue ink, and the scan does not resolve every name. The full chart is best read directly from the original. Waring), and the accompanying letter on pages 626–627.]*
