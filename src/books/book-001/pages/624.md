A large fold-out genealogical chart of the Perry family, headed **PERRY** at the upper left. The chart shows the line from a Barbadian Edward Perry of the 1680s settlement down to Amy FitzSimons's generation.

## Top-right note (in dark blue ink, in the compiler-cousin's hand)

> There are several Perrys living in St. Andrews & St. Pauls also St. Bartholomews Parishes — all of the same stock and possibly brothers — John, Edward, Benjamin and Peter. Mrs. [Thayton?] — "[Greats?]" came from Benjamin & her daughter Anne marrying Edward of the John line until Lewis [crossed out?] branches Perrys came from Barbados about 1680 where the family had first settled.
>
> — Joseph Door Waring
