I will probably go to an early service 7:30 or 8 o'clock Easter morning with Mickey, then have breakfast at some nice place with her — and in the afternoon go for a bus ride with the Edwards. — Monday is Libby's birthday, so I have tickets for the Theatre as her present. We all enjoy that more than anything else we can do.

I have my vacation in August this year — and will probably come straight to Charleston as I did last year for my visit with you — and then up to the mountains.

Lots and lots of love to you, old Sweetie —

**Ellen**
