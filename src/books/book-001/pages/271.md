  Christopher FitzSimons** (b. 24 Jan 1826; d. 14 May 1860 [pencilled
  death-year correction; he actually died 1866 in the Moss Grove
  tornado per p279]) and his wife **Susan Milliken Barker** (b. 23
  June 1827; d. 14 Dec 1900), with their seven numbered children and
  the next generation written across the page. Three photographs are
  mounted: upper-left oval portrait of Dr. Christopher FitzSimons (a
  bearded man, bust); upper-right oval portrait of Susan Milliken
  FitzSimons (seated, in white cap and dark dress); and a small lower
  carte-de-visite captioned "SUSAN MILLIKEN BARKER. Wife of Dr.
  CHRISTOPHER Fitz-Simons" showing her as a younger woman.
  The chart shows seven children: (1) Christopher (Jr.) "Kit", (2) Samuel Gaillard,

## Photograph captions

Upper-left oval photograph (bust portrait of a bearded man), captioned at top in cursive:

> Dr. Christopher Fitz-Simons

Upper-right oval photograph (seated woman in white head-dress, holding a book), captioned at top in cursive:

> Susan Milliken Fitz-Simons

Lower carte-de-visite (small bust portrait of a woman with head covering), captioned below:

> SUSAN MILLIKEN BARKER.
> Wife of.
> Dr. CHRISTOPHER Fitz-Simons
