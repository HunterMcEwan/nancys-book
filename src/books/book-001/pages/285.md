notes: "Third leaf of the multi-page handwritten letter from Susan M. FitzSimons to Mr. Osborne, numbered '3' at the top. Continues from page 284. She lists the kitchen items she wishes to sell with the stove (the tin ham pot bought separately is not included; the saucepans with handles she does not wish to sell), names $5 as the price for the things named with the piping, and asks Mr. Osborne's advice about whether the old cow had not better be sold this fall, about the calves to be sold this year, and asks him to send her any corn money he has and let her know what is not sold, as she will have taxes to pay."
