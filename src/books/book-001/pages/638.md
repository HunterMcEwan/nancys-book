**Packet at upper left (vertical pencil notation on the topmost folded sheet):**

> 1) Written by my Mother — Mary Annie Fitz Simons —
> about her father & grand father —
> 2) Extracts from the family Bible of John Hadley brother
> of M. A. F. S.'s grand mother Maria Ann Hadley.

*[The packet contains the loose sheets transcribed as pp. 633–637 (the compiler's mother Mary Anne "Minnie" Perry FitzSimons's 1924 narrative and her three-page transcription from the Hadley family Bible). Phase-1 had misread the cursive as "Written by my Auntie — Mrs. Minnie FitzSimons" but the relationship is "my Mother," confirming this is the compiler Amy's note about her own mother's writings. M.A.F.S. = Mary Anne (Perry) Fitz Simons.]*
