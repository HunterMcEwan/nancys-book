notes: "An album page showing three mounted items: a packet of folded family papers at the upper left (with vertical pencil notation on the topmost sheet), a single sheet of laid paper bearing a handwritten Perry/Drayton family tree at the upper right (held down with strips of yellow gummed tape at top and bottom, signed 'P.D.' in lower right corner), and a smaller card at the lower right with a list of Bessellieu family names. Extracts from the family Bible of John Hadley brother of M.A.F.S.'s grandmother Maria Ann Hadley'. The lower-right card is the family of Elizabeth Howell of Savannah Ga. who married Charles Mason Bessellieu of Grahamville, S.C.; their daughter Julia Virginia Bessellieu married B. F. Dunkin Perry. Key dates: Susannah Rawlins died July 20, 1797; Elizabeth Perry b. July 20, 1754; Susannah Perry Bapt March 26, 1747 / Died Oct 19, 1809 (per old Bible); Benjamin Perry (the elder) b. April 23, 1722. **Packet at upper left (vertical pencil notation on the topmost folded sheet):**

> 1) Written by my Mother — Mary Annie Fitz Simons —
> about her father & grand father —
> 2) Extracts from the family Bible of John Hadley brother
> of M. A. F. S.'s grand mother Maria Ann Hadley.

*[The packet contains the loose sheets transcribed as pp. 633–637 (the compiler's mother Mary Anne "Minnie" Perry FitzSimons's 1924 narrative and her three-page transcription from the Hedley family Bible — the surname canonical is **Hedley**, written here on Amy's pencil label as "Hadley," the same cursive H-e-d / H-a-d slip seen on p. 632). The relationship is "my Mother," confirming this is the compiler Amy's note about her own mother's writings. M.A.F.S. = Mary Anne (Perry) Fitz Simons.]*

*[The two strikethroughs ('Dunkin' over 'Mark Kirk', and 'Drane' over 'Dessallieu') are the writer's own mid-stream corrections. The final form names Benjamin Mark Kirk Perry m. Julia Dessallieu — but per the Bessellieu family list at lower right of this album page, the correct surname is **Bessellieu** (not Dessallieu). The standard family record for this marriage is **Benjamin Faneuil Dunkin Perry** (B. F. D. Perry, 1834–1874) m. **Julia Virginia Bessellieu** — i.e., the first strikethrough restores the canonical "Dunkin" middle name. Donkin Drayton Perry (B. F. D. Perry's brother) is a separate man, who married Jane Miller (per pp. 624 and 628).]*
