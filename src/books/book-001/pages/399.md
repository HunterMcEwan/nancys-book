Hand-written family record in blue ballpoint, centered on the page. The faint mirror-image writing behind it is the bleed-through of p398.

> **Mary Ann Walker** —
>
> Born June 8th 1918
> Died — Feb. 7 — 1975 —
>
> —m—
>
> **Oswald Beverly McEwan**
>
> 1) **Christopher Gaillard McEwan**
>    Born June 27th 1942 — m. **Cynthia Bleam** 1960
>
> 2) **Nancy Fletcher McEwan**
>    Born — Sept 13th 1946 — m. **Theodore Green** (1)
>    m. **Gary Norman** (2) Nov. 23 1975
>
> 3) **James Walker McEwan**
>    Born April 25th 1949 —

*[The faint mirror-image writing visible across this sheet is the reverse-image of the worksheet recto (p398), recording Mary Ann's elder siblings James Pickens Walker Jr. (1912–1969) and Emma Dee Walker Corbell (1915–1959), and the three Corbell children. The "Cynthia Bleam," "Theodore Green," and "Gary Norman" annotations on this side mark the marriages of the McEwan children; James Walker McEwan ("Jim") is Hunter McEwan's father.]*
