> This was repeated over and over to a monotonous tune and was sung to my father by his nurse — in the days when we owned our servants. My poor father had bought a rice plantation in **Pee-dee**. It was near one owned by Judge **Dunkin**. He was his intimate friend [illegible] when my father was nursed. My grandfather contracted pneumonia crossing **Pee-dee** river one bitterly cold night and died shortly after this purchase. They spent the summer before my father's birth in a pineland in **Williamsburg** in an ordinary unsealed [illegible] house.
