The second sheet of Nancy's letter, in the same blue ballpoint hand. Continues from page 410.

> imagined it would be. I had so much fun playing in it.
>
> I've been dating several different people, but no one in particular. I've been out with Pete Perry from Atlanta several times and I really do like him. His family is from Charleston and his grandfather is the sheriff there now. His name is Chester Perry. Crissy and Bo know them real well and I thought maybe you would too. Other than Pete I haven't been interested in any of the boys I've been out with.
>
> I talked to Momma and Daddy yesterday and they both seemed fine. They are going to New Orleans this Thursday. I don't know when I am going to get a chance to go home. I will get no semester break whatsoever. I keep looking for a ride, but the ones to Orlando are few and far between.
>
> Please tell Dick and Ann that I have written them, but I have received no address. I really haven't forgotten all about them.
>
> I'll write you again as soon as I can.
>
> Much love,
> Nancy
