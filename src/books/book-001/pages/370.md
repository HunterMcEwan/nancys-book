Second sheet of the same letter, continuing the blue-ink cursive from page 369.

> him & each time he tried to get out his wake, so he slept all night in the crib.
>
> He got an offer at Palatka but after investigating Hudson he decided to stay where he is. His future looked brighter there.
>
> We leave Sat. week for a week at Petersburg. Really dread to go this year. I will stay home my second week & try to rest. Home. Raymond went back to work Mon. — haven't heard but hope things went well.
>
> I miss news of Minnie thru you. Had a note before you left but nothing since. Any prospects if she has any [news] for her house. She said something about going to Orlando but have forgotten when.
>
> So sorry we will miss Louisa in Virginia but so glad she can come there.
>
> Love to you — wish we could stop by this year & see you —
>
> Mary
