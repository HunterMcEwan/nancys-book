> Emma Dee died May, 1959, and Dr. Corbell died the following [*the 'following' is overstruck on the typewriter — the typist appears to have hit a stray key on top of 'ol' before continuing*] January, 1960.
>
> Mary Ann married attorney, O.B. McEwan of Orlando, Florida in 1941. They had three children — Christopher G. McEwan, Nancy Fletcher McEwan and James Walker McEwan. Mary Ann died February 7, 1975.

*[The lower two-thirds of the sheet is occupied by a faint mirror-offset of typing transferred from a facing page; it duplicates passages of the Samuel Cadwalder Walker biography that appears in clean form on page 662 and is not transcribed here.]*
