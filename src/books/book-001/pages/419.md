  An album page with two pasted items. Upper centre: a typewritten 'order' on white paper mounted on a pale-blue paper backing, styled as a mock court decree (printed but never filed) from the **North Valley Hospital, Flathead County, Montana** announcing the birth of 'Baby Boy McEwan' on 23 October 1974 and decreeing he 'shall be forever known as **CHRISTOPHER MICKLER McEWAN**.' Parents named are **Chris G. McEwan and Cynthia E. Wade Covill, M.D. Lower left: a small French air-mail envelope franked with five 0.60-franc green Marianne (Bequet) stamps cancelled at **58 NEVERS GARE / NIÈVRE, 12-4-1976**, addressed in blue ballpoint to **Mrs. J.   The "③" at the upper left of the address block is the sender's own enumeration — Dee and Claude Gramond apparently mailed three (or more) identical announcements to American family. The recipient, **Ann Seymour Knight Walker** ("Mrs. James Pickens Walker Jr."), was the compiler Amy's daughter-in-law (wife of Amy's son Jim Walker Jr.) and Dee Corbell's aunt-by-marriage. The card enclosed in this envelope is mounted on the facing page 420.

An album page with two pasted items, an ocean apart in geography but linked thematically — both announce great-grandchildren of the compiler Amy FitzSimons Walker (Christopher Mickler McEwan via her daughter Mary Ann, Eric Gramond via her daughter Emma Dee).

**Upper item — mock 'order' decree (white paper on pale-blue backing):**

> IN THE NORTH VALLEY HOSPITAL OF THE STATE OF MONTANA
> IN AND FOR THE COUNTY OF FLATHEAD
>
> In The Matter of            )
>                             )    O R D E R
> BABY BOY McEWAN,            )
>                             )
>
> This Baby coming on regularly for delivery this 23rd day of October, 1974, and said Baby Boy being represented by his parents, Chris G. McEwan and Cynthia E. McEwan; and evidence having been introduced by and on behalf of said Baby Boy, to-wit:
>
> Weight — 7 lbs. 12½ oz.
> Length — 21"
>
> IT IS HEREBY ORDERED, ADJUDGED AND DECREED:
>
> 1. That said Baby Boy shall be forever known as CHRISTOPHER MICKLER McEWAN.
>
> 2. That said CHRISTOPHER MICKLER McEWAN be entitled to all of lifes love, happiness, and successes.
>
> DATED this 23rd day of October, 1974.
>
> *[signed]* R. Wade Covill, M.D.
> Attending Physician
> R. Wade Covill, M.D.

**Lower item — French air-mail envelope:**

A small white envelope, **PAR AVION** stamped in blue at the upper left, franked with five green 0.60-franc Marianne (Bequet) stamps and cancelled with a circular date stamp reading **58 NEVERS GARE / NIÈVRE, 20⁴⁵ / 12-4-1976**. Addressed in blue ballpoint:

> ③
>
> Mrs. J. P. Walker Jr.
> 4275 Garibaldi Ave.
> Jacksonville, Fla. 32210
> USA

Pencilled annotation along the lower edge in the compiler's hand:

> Birth announcement of Eric Gramond.
