A pale envelope, photographed from the back. A Jacksonville, Fla. circular postmark dated 1958 is at the upper right; cancellation bars extend along the top edge. The flap is unsealed and slightly torn at the upper right.

Along the lower edge (upside-down relative to the postmark), in pencil:

> Hancock River Hous[e]
> Hancock, Va.
