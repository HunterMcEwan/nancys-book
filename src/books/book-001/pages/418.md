A translucent printed wedding invitation, black type centered on pale blue-green stock.

> Gloria Mercedes Gutierrez
>
> and
>
> Robert Lawrence Corbell
>
> would be honored by your presence at their wedding ceremony to be held at one o'clock, on the afternoon of December eighteenth, nineteen-seventy one
>
> on the steps of Alta Vista Park, at the foot of Pierce Street, San Francisco.
>
> Reception immediately following at 2621 Laguna Street
>
> In the event of rain, the ceremony will be held at the above address
