A child's drawing in pink colored pencil and foil stars. The pink crayon fills the entire square; over it, a darker pink line traces a cross or quartered-banner motif with a scalloped or perforated border around the outer edge. Five metallic foil stars are stuck on at five positions of the composition (silver upper left, blue upper right, large red center, blue lower left, silver lower right).
