## Left page

> Vans' maid — was a great help & comfort — I had never any other than a feeling of repulsion for Flora, though I had never seen her — but no one could have been more quiet, efficient, & devoted in Mother's chamber. She seemed to take in Mother's needs, in the most delicate way, & could not be persuaded to leave to rest, day or night — We had offers of help on all sides, & every sort of kindness in word & deed, from friends, & mere acquaintances, & the doctors were devoted — Her pulse gave then hopes till the last.
