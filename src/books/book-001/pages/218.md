A narrow vertical strip — the back/outer panel of the folded Notary Protest, scanned a second time. Most of the strip is blank. Faint mirror-image bleed-through from the printed face is visible behind the ink.

In cursive ink at the top:

> Bern\[ar]d E Bee
> Nov 1/4    100 —

Below, a flourishing signature:

> Thomas Milliken
