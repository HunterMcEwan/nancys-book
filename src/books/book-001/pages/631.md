> **Amy Perry FitzSimons**, married, at home in **Adams Run, S.C.**, by the Rev. **Thomas P. Baker**, on the 20th of October 1908 to **James Pickens Walker**, of **Philippi**, **West Virginia**.
>
> Their child is
>
> **Amy Perry** — born 20th of March 1910. on Sunday at 11.30 P.M.
>
> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Died at Weldon, N.C.
>
> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; age 14 mos.
>
> Born Charleston, S.C. January 5th 1912 *[in pencil:]* 2 P.M.
>
> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; **James Pickens Walker, Jr.**

*[The opening line reads "Their child is" in the singular — the page was written when Amy Perry the elder daughter was still the only child; the second entry recording James Pickens Walker Jr.'s 5 January 1912 birth was added afterward in the same hand, and the pencilled "2 P.M." beneath the date was added later still.]*
