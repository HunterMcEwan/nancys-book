> always — She it thought her so much better on Thursday that he did not go to see her on Tuesday — at [Sunrise?] she [felt?] what she had grieved & I did not know what to do. I told him to go in the afternoon & yesterday she was [translated?] — It is very disheartening & I hope no more of them will take it for it seems impossible for them to recover. — [Lyul?] said when I think of your aunts [bonbeings?] — & no help for it — & all the [trouble?] added to [our?] poor little — and so are all — a troubled people — devilly & indirectly we can trace it to the war. — [Slater?] has gone to buy a [pkg?] of them from a [wagon?] at the [door] — I [wish?] one would a [pack?] of them so the dear little [fellows?] not Mr. [Stragyll?] a Lavender Bay inside this for Mrs Ellen drawer — All them all for us — Tuli & [Adela?] go to Flat Rock [to day?] — Mary came down on Tuesday with Campbell & Kirkwood — The two ladies went yesterday, to Charleston — Mary Louisa & Gannie return on Saturday & Mary had to send on these dentist — She looks very pretty — but is not strong — Mr. Huger & Col.
