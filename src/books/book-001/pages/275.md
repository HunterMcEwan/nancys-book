## Annotations at top right of an otherwise blank sheet

> W. H. & T. G. B.
>
> Gen Hampton

*[Remainder of the page is blank.]*

*[Cover sheet for the multi-page eulogy of **Dr. Christopher FitzSimons (3rd)** (d. 17 May 1866 in the Moss Grove tornado), composed jointly by his nephew/son-in-law **W. Huger FitzSimons** and his brother-in-law **Theodore Gaillard Barker** ("T. G. B.", Hampton Legion adjutant and Charleston attorney). Gen. Wade Hampton's separate tribute — referenced on the companion slip mounted on p. 272 — is also part of the bundle, which is bound out of order across pp. 273-278.]*
