> 11/8/18
>
> Dear Hugh,
>
> I have put my name opposite the two pieces that **Mamie** expressed a wish that she would like to have if it [does] not interfere with any one [else's] plans, [and] it but if it does just say nothing about it but let me down for any thing else or in [any] [way] does [not] make any difference to me. I think you will agree about The Farm for Sis & I know that you are a great help to **Aunt [Lillian]** in her moving it was a sad breaking up for her at her age.
>
> Truly yours,
> **S. G. FitzSimons**
