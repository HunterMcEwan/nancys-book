Va., copied 'To Whom It May Concern' and addressed to Mrs. James Walker Modisette as the great-grandniece. Acknowledged before W. D. Corder, Notary Public, with his stamp at lower right. Anna Walker Modisette has signed in blue ink above the notarization."

A typed family-record affidavit on plain paper.

*[Typed upper right:]*

> 28 September 1953
> Philippi, W. Va.

*[Typed body:]*

> To Whom It May Concern:
>
> The following record of births and deaths, etc., is copied from the family Bible belonging to Mr. and Mrs. James Pickens, Philippi, W. Va., (James Pickens was the son of Alexander Pickens):

*[Tabular section, headings:]*

> | BORN | DIED | AGE | Relationship of each to Mrs. Anna Walker Modisette |
> | James Pickens | Jan. 5, 1817 | Jan. 22, 1887 | Grandfather |
> | Anne M. Pickens | July 31, 1825 | May 11, 1899 | Grandmother |
> | Alexander H. Pickens | Nov. 19, 1842 | Oct. 12, 1868 | Uncle |
> | John D. Pickens | Jan. 1, 1845 | Dec. — | 1916 | Uncle |
> | Rachel Marg-ret Pickens | Nov. 8, 1849 | — | — | Aunt |
> | Charity M. Pickens | Jan. 12, 1854 | — | — | Aunt |
> | Emma D. Pickens | March 10, 1856 | Mch. 30, 1933 | Mother |
> | Fanny Virginia Pickens | Sept. 23, 1858 | Feb. 7, 1949 | Aunt |
> | Mollie Pickens | Oct. 8, 1860 | Oct. 1, 1949 | Aunt |
> | Davor Pickens | Sept. 18, 1863 | Sept. 26, 1945 | Uncle |

> (The above eight persons were the children of Mr. and Mrs. Jas. Pickens.)

*[Continued:]*

> Alexander Pickens — Apr. 2, 1747 — Great-Grandfather
> Margaret Pickens (his wife) — Dec. 18, 1863 — Great-Grandmother

> The following is the family of Mrs. Anne M. Pickens (Mrs. James Pickens):
>
> James Dever, 1788 — July 28, 1866 — Great-Grandfather *[interlined: 'b. 1786 7-28-66']*
> Rachel Dever (his wife), 1777 — June 18, 1836 — Great-Grandmother *[interlined: 57 Yrs. old]*
> John Dever (their son) — May 29, 1846 — Great Uncle
> Chairty Dever Nichol (Daughter) — Sept. 23, 1899 — Great Aunt

> Rebecca Stewart Pickens — wife of Alexander Pickens — brother of James Pickens. May 6, 1852 Age 32 Yrs.

*[Signed in blue ink:]*

> Anna Walker Modisette

*[Typed beneath:]*

> Acknowledged before the subscriber, this 28th day of September 1953.

*[Notary seal and signature, lower right:]*

> W. D. Corder
> Notary Public
> My Commission Expires January 15, 1960
