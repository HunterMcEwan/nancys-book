notes: "Second scan of the multi-page July 1853 Sullivan's Island letter. Two facing pages of cursive ink writing, both heavily filled. The left page describes the writer's headaches, illness, and improvements to the house and lower floor (carpenters from the plantation, a new dining room with pantry, lodgings for boys/Claudian). The right page describes purchasing second-hand cottage furniture — bureau, marble-top, French bedstead, washstand, bedsteads, mattresses, an old centre table, a camphine lamp converted to spirit gas, and an account of the heat and the new omnibus to come out for ladies."
