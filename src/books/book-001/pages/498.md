*[Continued from page 497.]*

> the medical schools at the Sorbonne & U. of Dublin were the best anywhere. I had a French Creole woman for my nurse & mother said that he liked to discuss French cookery with her. Mother also said that he liked to tell me that when life was nice over he would take me to Paris & buy beautiful dresses there for me etc. etc.
>
> I have enjoyed the peach preserves that you sent me so much but I am not sure that my thanks got to you. I still have some.
>
> If you & Anne & Pickens are ever here, day or night, I am at home & come & see me. With much love for all —
>
> Aunt Ellen
>
> P.S. The place for you in the N.C. Mts. sought by Louisa is secure & arranged for. — E.M.F.
