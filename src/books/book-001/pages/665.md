notes: "A printed promotional / order-form flyer issued by the Barbour County Historical Society in 1979, soliciting submissions and pre-orders for 'THE HISTORY OF BARBOUR COUNTY, W.VA.' for delivery in 1979. The upper half of the flyer pictures sample county history books (the displayed volume is labeled MANISTEE — MASON — OCEANA, a Central Michigan county history used here as a stand-in cover image) and describes the planned 9 × 12 leatherette binding with gold-stamped cover. The lower half is a perforated BOOK ORDER BLANK with line items for the \$25.00 book, \$0.75 tax, \$2.00 postage (if mailed), and gift certificates, with mailing address Barbour County History Book, Philippi, W.Va. 26416 and a 'DEADLINE: MARCH 30, 1979' boxed at upper right. Pasted into the album because the compilers of pages 660–664 contributed family histories to this volume."

A printed flyer on cream paper.

> THE HISTORY OF BARBOUR COUNTY, W. VA.
>
> is being compiled by
>
> BARBOUR COUNTY HISTORICAL SOCIETY
>
> for delivery in 1979

A photograph fills the middle of the flyer, showing two sample county-history volumes — one open, one upright displaying a gold-stamped cover lettered

> HISTORY
>
> MANISTEE · MASON · OCEANA
>
> CENTRAL MICHIGAN
>
> ILLUSTRATED

Adjacent text reads:

> This limited edition will be a collectors item which will be treasured by current and future family members! They will make excellent gifts for Holidays, Birthdays or Anniversaries. Gift certificates are now available!

> It will be 9 × 12, beautifully bound in hardback leatherette with a gold-stamped cover!

Beneath the photo, a caption:

> Pictured here are county history books which are similar to Barbour County's upcoming publication.
