A pale cream business-size envelope mounted alone on the album sheet. Through the thin paper the back of a cursive letter shows faintly in mirror-image.

Addressed in blue fountain-pen cursive:

> Mrs. J. P. Walker
> Route 1 Box 401
> Hendersonville, N.C.

Postmark and stamp at upper right:

> JACKSONVILLE 4 FLA.
> JUN 7 12:30 PM 1963
>
> *[Slogan cancellation:]* "Always Mail for Better Business Service"
>
> *[5-cent stamp depicting the U.S. flag and the White House.]*
