Two oval studio portraits mounted at the top of the page; a handwritten descendant chart fills the lower portion.

The LEFT portrait caption (blue ballpoint beneath the mat) reads:

> **Anne Dever Pickens**
> B. July 31st 1825 — m — D. May 11th 1899

The RIGHT portrait caption reads:

> **James Pickens**
> B. Jan 5th 1817 — m — D. Jan 22nd 1887

*[The portraits are of the compiler's husband's maternal grandparents — James Pickens of West Virginia (1817–1887) and his wife Anne Maria Dever (1825–1899). Their daughter Emma Dee Pickens (b. 1856) married Samuel Walker, and her son James Pickens Walker married the compiler Amy FitzSimons.]*
