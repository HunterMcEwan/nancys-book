Two oval studio portraits mounted at the top of the page; a handwritten descendant chart fills the lower portion. The left portrait is captioned in blue ballpoint beneath the mat:

> **Anne Dever Pickens**
> B. July 6th 1813 — M. — D. June 26th 1899

The right portrait is captioned:

> **James Pickens**
> B. Janr 5th 1788 — M. — D. Nov 22nd 1867

**Pencil descendant chart, beneath the portraits — left side (Pickens line):**

> **Alexander Pickens** — D. April 6th 1797
> *m.*
> **Margaret Wiley** — D. Dec. 16th 1863
> *m.*
> **James Pickens** B. Jan 5th 1788 — D. Janr 5th 1867
> *m.*
> **Anne Marinder Dever** B. July 31st 1818 — D. May 11th 1899

**Children of James Pickens and Anne Marinder Dever (column headings across):**

> Alexander — John — Margaret — Charity — Emma Dee — Virginia [Dee] — Mollie — Davor [Dever]
> *(beneath each, descendant lines):*
>
> Alexander Pickens: Mary, Galloway, Pearl, Walter
> John: Addie McKee Boling
> Margaret: m. Howard Young; Marie, Leila Davis, Rosa, Sterling
