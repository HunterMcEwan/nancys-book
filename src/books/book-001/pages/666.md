A single printed sheet, the top half a typewritten outline and the bottom half three large-type promotional banners.

> Items to consider in writing your
>
> Family Household History

> I. When and why your family came to the county
>
> 1. How old were you (if you weren't born here)
> 2. Include as many generations as you can.
> 3. Where did they learn of the County.
> 4. Where did they settle.
> 5. Your Childhood: health, accidents, playmates, trips, brothers and sisters, unusual happenings, visitors, relatives you remember, factors that influenced your lifestyle.
> 6. Schooldays: Schools attended, teachers, special activities, achievements, humorous situations, who or what influenced you most.

> II. Activities:
>
> 1. Jobs: What did your ancestors do for a living. What is your present occupation.
> 2. Sports.
> 3. Transportation in your family.
> 4. Courtship and Marriage: where you met, proposal, wedding parties, honeymoon, meeting your in-laws.
> 5. Married life: settling down, your new home, housekeeping, joys and sorrows.

> III. Avocations:
>
> 1. What do you do for recreation.
> 2. Civic and Political Activities.
> 3. Church: Activities, Influence.
> 4. Hobbies.
> 5. Travels.
> 6. Family landmarks and historical sites.

> IV. Remembrance:
>
> 1. Special celebrations.
> 2. Holidays, birthdays, etc.
> 3. Ancestors: impressions of those you knew and heard about.
> 4. Hardships.
> 5. Humorous Incidents. Frightening Experiences.

> V. Writing tips:
>
> 1. Tell what is interesting and meaningful to you.
> 2. You will not be graded. This is not a test!
> 3. Any story that does not embarrass or hurt someone is acceptable.
> 4. If your story is not included, the History of our County will not be complete.
> 5. DO IT NOW!

Three large-type promotional banners at the foot of the sheet:

> **REMEMBER THE DEADLINE IS MARCH 30, 1979**

> **There is no charge for writing your Family Household History!**

> **However, you may order as many books as you like!**
