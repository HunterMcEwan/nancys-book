The closing leaf of the letter. The upper half of the sheet is blank.

*[Continuation from page 477.]*

… the point where *"Papa don't know."*

Uncle Theodore looks remarkably well but pines for the Mrs. Minnie has been sick but is better today. Brother was in town Sunday; he looks well and you will agree with me in thinking it is always nice to have him around. Give my love to Sister and I hope she has recovered from her cold —

> Your aff son
> W. Huger FitzSimons
