He was invited to dine, & take tea, & came home with Capt *[Sharpe]*, who was to join his Company, not far from here. They came in Capt *[Sharpe's]* carriage, & did not get here till 12 o'clock at night, having started after dark. M\[r]s. *[Sharpe]* and Lizzie *[Hayer]* — half-sister of W & P Wayne — Father is delighted with *[him]*.
