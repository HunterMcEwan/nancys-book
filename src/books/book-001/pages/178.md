A pre-printed legal form on heavy paper, the printed text of which is supplemented in flowing ink in several blanks. A printed brace at upper left frames the case caption.

> The State of South Carolina,
>
> COUNTY OF *Berkeley*

> *Theodore G. Barker as Executor of Will of John B. Milliken and in his own right*
>
> Plaintiff
>
> *against*
>
> *Susan M. FitzSimons, Ellen Porcher Thomas M. Barker, William I. Barker, Henrietta Catherine Barker and Joe Joiner*
>
> Defendant*s*

At upper right:

> COURT OF COMMON PLEAS.
>
> COPY SUMMONS. FOR RELIEF.
>
> *(Complaint Served.)*

Beneath, in larger pre-printed text and ink:

> To the Defendant *Thomas M. Barker*
>
> YOU ARE HEREBY SUMMONED and required to answer the complaint in this action, of which a copy is herewith served upon you, and to serve a copy of your answer to the said complaint on the subscriber at *his* office *16 Broad Street Charleston, S.C.* ______ within twenty days after the service hereof, exclusive of the day of such service; and if you fail to answer the complaint within the time aforesaid, the plaintiff in this action will apply to the Court for the relief demanded in the complaint.

> Dated *fifteenth July* A.D. 18*90*

Signed in ink at lower right:

> D B Gilliland
>
> *Plaintiff's Attorney*
