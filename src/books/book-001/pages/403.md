notes: "A pre-printed birth certificate form from the City of Charleston, S.C. Department of Health, numbered 77/204, recording the birth of Amy Fitzsimons Walker on March 20, 1910 at Roper Hospital, daughter of Amy Pickens Fitzsimons and James P. Walker. The form was certified as a true copy on Nov. 22, 1940 and bears the embossed seal and signature of Leon Banov, M.D., Health Officer. The 'Walker' surname here is the child's; given the date and parents, the subject is the future Mary Ann Walker McEwan's older sister, the daughter of the album's compiler."

A printed Department of Health certificate filled in by typewriter. At the upper left a circular seal: **City of Charleston, 1670**. Beneath the seal: *Leon Banov, M.D. / Health Officer.*

> **City of Charleston, S.C.**
> **Department of Health** No. 77/204
>
> **Return of a Birth**
> Date filed: March 23, 1910 — 19—
>
> 1. Full name of child (if any): **Amy Fitzsimons Walker**
> 2. Sex: **Female** — No. of child of mother: **First**
> 3. Race or color: **White** — Date of Birth: **March 20, 1910**
>    (if not of white race) [blank]
> 4. Place of birth: **Roper Hospital**
> 5. Full name of mother: **Amy Fitzsimons Walker**
>    (Maiden name): **Amy Pickens Fitzsimons**
> 6. Mother's birthplace: **S. C.**
> 7. Mother's residence: **N. C.**
> 8. Full name of father: **James P. Walker**
> 9. Father's occupation: **Civil Engineer** — Birthplace: **Va.**
> 10. Name & address of attendant: **Allen J. Jervey, M. D.**

Stamped diagonally across the right side of the form in red ink:

> This is a correct copy from the original record. NOV 22 1940
>
> *[Signed]* Leon Banov, M. D.
> Health Officer

*[The given name of the mother is written here as 'Amy Fitzsimons Walker'; her maiden name in the next line is 'Amy Pickens Fitzsimons'. This is the album compiler.]*
