> **Walker, James Pickens,** General Superintendent, Atlantic Coast Line R. R., Office 18 Jefferson St., Jacksonville, Fla. *Born:* Overland, W. Va., April 28, 1885. *Son of* Samuel C. and Emma H. (Pickens) Walker. *Married:* Amy Fitz Simons, Oct. 26, 1908. *Children:* J. P., Jr., Dec.; Mary Ann. *Education:* public schools, Rockville Academy, Washington and Lee Univ. *Entered Railway Service:* July 1903, as chainman, Chesapeake & Ohio, serving in this capacity to office assistant Asst. Engineer, Jan. 1906, entered service of Atlantic Coast Line, as transitman 1 May 1, 1907; was successively roa, eng., div. engr., asst. engr., asst. supt. and supt. at Charleston, S. C.; Apr. 1, 1923-Sept. 1, 1928, Supt. of transp., Jacksonville, Fla.; Sept., 1928-Oct. 1934, Gen. Supt. Savannah, Ga.; Nov., 1934, present position. *Office:* 18 Jefferson St., Jacksonville, Fla. *Was twin. mgr. for the U. S. R. R. Administration.* *Memberships:* Clubs and *fraternities:* Delta Tau Delta; Washington Society of Engineers; A.S.C.E.; A.R.E.A.; Sigma Xi; Tau Beta Pi.
