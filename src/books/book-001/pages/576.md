**Newspaper clipping (upper left), with two-column photograph captioned beneath the picture:**

> L. W. BARRETT (LEFT), D. McK. ALLSTON JR.
> New Vice President And New Trust Officer

> **Barrett, Allston Win Promotions At C&S**
>
> Lawrence W. Barrett has been named vice president and trust officer and Donald McKay Allston Jr. has been named trust officer of the Citizens & Southern National Bank.
>
> The announcement was made following the regular meeting of the board of directors at the [annual] stockholders meeting.
>
> The promotions reflect the growth of the C&S trust department and its increasing importance in the operation of the bank in Charleston, a bank spokesman said.
>
> Barrett, trust officer of the bank since 1948, had served previously in various official capacities in the commercial department. He is a native of Brockton, Mass. After he was graduated from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in 1937, he served in the U. S. Army. He was separated from the service in Charleston in 1948 and has made this city his home since [that time…]

> L. W. Barrett Jr. of Charleston and the Rev. Harold E. Barrett of Lookout Mountain, Tenn.
>
> Allston, the son of Donald McKay Allston and Mary FitzSimons Allston of Charleston, was educated in local schools and at St. Paul's in Concord, N. H.
>
> **CITADEL GRADUATE**
>
> He was graduated from The Citadel in 1948 and received his law degree from the University of South Carolina in 1951. He came to work for the C&S Bank in 1951 and was made assistant trust officer in 1952.
>
> He is a member of the Carolina Yacht Club, the Phi Delta Phi legal fraternity and the South Carolina Bar Assn. He and his wife, the former Miss Marguerita FitzSimons of Hendersonville, N.C., attend the Holy Trinity Protestant Episcopal Church. They and their three children live at 825 Wappoo Dr., Riverland Terrace.
>
> **GROW THIRD OF CORN**
