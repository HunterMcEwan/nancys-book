A printed birth-return form with the City of Charleston seal at the upper left, filled in by typewriter.

> **CITY OF CHARLESTON, S.C.**
>
> **DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH**
>
> **RETURN OF A BIRTH**
>
> Date filed **February 14, 1888**   No. **27-27**
>
> LEON BANOV, M.D.
> HEALTH OFFICER
>
> 1. Full name of child   **Amy Perry FitzSimons**
> 2. Sex **Girl**   : Number child of mother — Race **White**
> 3. Date of birth **Feb. 4, 1888**   : Place of birth **4 Greenhill St.**
> 4. Full name of mother **Minnie W. FitzSimons** — Maiden Name **Perry**   Residence **4 Greenhill St.**
> 5. Mother's birthplace **Barnwell**
> 6. Full name of father **Samuel FitzSimons**
> 7. Father's birthplace **St. John**   Occupation **Rice Pl[anter]**
> 8. Name and address of attendant **Jno. L. Ancrum**   *name added by affid. 1-28-50

*[The form is a 1950 certified copy of the 1888 birth record — the printed name "Leon Banov, M.D." identifies the Charleston Health Officer of that later period. The notation at the lower right indicates Amy's full given name "Amy Perry FitzSimons" was supplied by affidavit on January 28, 1950.]*
