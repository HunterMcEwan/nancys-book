A hand-drawn pencil pedigree chart, faint and rubbed in places. Left-to-right generation flow, with curly brackets grouping spouses to their children. The chart records the seven children of **Mary Anne Perry** (the compiler's mother "Minnie") and **Samuel Gaillard FitzSimons** Sr. — that is, **the compiler Amy and her siblings** — together with their respective spouses and grandchildren.

>
> **Samuel Gaillard FitzSimons**

**Top center, a small bracketed group annotated *"died in early childhood":***

> **Dunkin Perry** *[pencil capital reads as "H" but is Amy's open-top D — cf. the canonical family-Bible record on [p630](/books/book-001/630/) and Amy's memoir [Book 2, p7](/books/book-002/007/)]*
>
> **Thomas Porcher**
>
> **Samuel Cashell** *[= Samuel Cashel FitzSimons]*
