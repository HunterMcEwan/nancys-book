A handwritten letter sheet, oriented landscape, in cursive ink. Above the page number is the annotation "overlooked this page." The page is numbered **(6)** at top center.

> *overlooked this page*
>
> (6)
>
> to go to see her — I hope the little thing is getting on
> Dr. Huger is still practicing, & looking handsome in
> his grey beard. — Aunt Ellinor is still lively, &
> has the little portrait of "Nelly Cordes" hanging
> in her room. — It belongs to Uncle Theodore, so
> you will see it "next time you come" — Do
> you go to Church — and where? — I know you
> see Mary Campbell sometimes. Do give her
> my love — By the time you get to the
> end of this page you will draw a long
> breath and take a rest. Good night my
> dear, Your aff Aunt Ellen.
