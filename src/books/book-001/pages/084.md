The concluding portion of a handwritten letter in brown ink. The closing paragraph occupies the top of the sheet; the signature, date, and a list of orders with dollar amounts follow. The lower two-thirds of the sheet are blank except for show-through of writing on the verso.

> says he will take down some cotton for me this trip which will land at or near you, at Dukes's. For your House it will be marked **CC**. — I will get him off as soon as possible.
>
> yours sincerely
>
> T. [Llowndi]
>
> 30th Jan'y 1857.
>
> Orders
>
> On or [Gaillard] & Snowden Jr. — $240
>
> Ingraham & Webb. — 50
>
> W. C. Dukes. — 323.90
>
> D[itt]o. Felder Selber — 500
>
> $1123.90
