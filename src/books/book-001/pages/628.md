A second leaf of the fold-out Perry family tree, continuing the chart from page 624. Drawn in light pencil with names bracketed in by curly braces and connected by thin lines.

The upper half develops the **Waring** line: **Anna Perry** × **Lewis A. Waring**, with their children **Jane Lesesne Waring** (m. **Dr. John Aurey**), **Wayne Waring** (m. **\[Eulen?] Lauren\[?]**), **Edward A. Perry Waring** (m. **Ann Waters**), **Eliza Waring**, **Jennie Waring**, **Joseph Ioor Waring** (m. **Camilla Faber**) — issue **Joseph Ioor Waring** \[the younger] and **Dorothy Waring** — and on through several further generations.

The lower half restates the line of **Edward A. Perry & his wife / Maria Ann Mauger née Medley** and brackets in their issue:

- **Maria Ann**
- **Benjamin Faneuil Sumter Dunkin Perry** \[m. **Julia Virginia Bessellieu**] — issue **Hunter Perry**, **David Perry**, **Julia Virginia**, **Charles Mason Bessellieu**, etc.
- **Mary Ann Perry** \[m. **Edward Jackson FitzSimons**]
- **Elizabeth Howell** \[m. **William Bass Coffin**] — issue **Hunter Perry**, **David Perry**, **Julia Virginia**, **Charles Mason**, **Bessellieu \[Coffin?]**

Further collateral notes at the bottom margin name **James W. Doar**, an **Annie Drayton \[?]** line, **Jane Walker**, **Theo. \[illegible]**, and **Anderson \[Banfast?]**.

*[The full chart is closely written in light pencil and a literal name-by-name transcription is not feasible at the resolution of this scan. The sheet is best read from the original. See the related cover sheet on page 623, the attribution on page 625, and the accompanying letter on pages 626–627.]*
