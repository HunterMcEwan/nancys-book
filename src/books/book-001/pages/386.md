Printed letterhead at the top center:

> WOOD GROVE FARM
> JERSEY, VIRGINIA

Body, in dark ink, in a large hand:

> This is to certify that on October 20th, 1908, at 8:30 p.m. I united **Amy Perry FitzSimons** and **James Pickens Walker** of Philippi, West Virginia, in marriage. — This marriage took place at the residence of the bride at *[struck through: "Adams"]* Adams Run, South Carolina, while the undersigned was the Rector of Christ Church parish in that Diocese.
>
> **Thomas Perry Baker**
> Jersey, Virginia
