The page is arranged around a central column of large head-and-shoulders childhood portraits of the four Walker children. A vintage studio portrait of Amy Perry FitzSimons (the compiler's mother) holding a mandolin sits at the upper left. A formal late-life portrait of James Pickens Walker in coat and tie is at the upper right. Across the bottom edge are smaller snapshots of the same children grown, a 1917 St. Cecilia Society calling card, and a newspaper obituary clipping for J. P. Walker.
