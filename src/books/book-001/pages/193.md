## Left column

> we cared talk about this any other time — Miss Louisa flew to the garden & remained with [Hib], & I laughed — Nell asked me quite Sternly later in the Evening, "Hadnt I been waiting longer for Louisa?". So then I had the fun of telling the joke on Miss Louisa. So you See we have our little jokes Still —
>
> Mr. McMillan & Hennie came before dark & Hennie was taken up by Louisa to her room. He was determined with it & Every thing in it. they left and after a while Mitchell & Hennie came & after they left we all went [pted]. The Reception day Nell & I scurried around & people sent beautiful flowers — arranged & some basket of Cut flowers

## Right column

> & Saw how late Aunt Louisa could be to breakfast. It was a very windy day So they went to Broad St. After Church & then [fast] home to dinner — Mr. Pinckney & Gen. Conner came to Tell down Stairs took a Cigar & had a few [words] I suppose down the Lounge in the room while Louisa received Miss [Mindon] up Stairs in the drawing Room — Nell went to Church — I did not, as it was So windy & She gives a funny account of how She was Stared at by every one Men, women, & Children & how disappointed they looked to See her Small Self in the middle of the pew — little dry me
