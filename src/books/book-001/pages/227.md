A white envelope. The address is handwritten in blue ink in a flowing cursive hand:

> Mrs. J. P. Walker
>
> Hendersonville
>
> N. C.

Below at lower left:

> Route 2.

Postal markings at the upper right: a circular black postmark reading:

> **COLUMBIA**
> **MAY 3**
> **11 30 AM**
> **1962**
> **S. C.**

Adjacent to the postmark is a slogan cancellation depicting the Liberty Bell (with the letters **MH** inside the bell) reading:

> SUPPORT YOUR
> MENTAL HEALTH
> ASSOCIATION

A 4-cent violet Lincoln U.S. postage stamp is affixed at the upper right corner.
