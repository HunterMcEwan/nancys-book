[Main letter text, beginning at top right:]

> Sullivans Island —
> July 5th / 53
>
> This is, as you will perceive My dear
> Mollie the anniversary of our Indepen-
> -dence, this day last year we all spent
> in a merry way at the Moultrie House.
> I have just moved down here & am not
> yet settled, but it is such an immense
> relief to get from town — The weather has
> been perfectly intense, & very debilitating
> for we have not had any rain to speak of
> for nearly 3 months consequently every thing
> is burned up and rain water is at a
> premium — I have received both
> your nice & interesting letters to me,
> & Aunt Kate has sent me the family
> epistles to read — I really think you
> first rate as a correspondent, which is
> more than I can say of myself, but I
> have been in bed with dreadful headaches
> such as I had last year before I came
