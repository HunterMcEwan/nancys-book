*[Continued from page 495.]*

> the clan! I think I have to thank you my dear for the box of delightful jars of guava which I have enjoyed so much. When you sent them before. They are not only delightful to eat but very nice to have on hand when Company turns up.
>
> I got such a nice card & note from your Mary Anne. I wish that I could see the developement of your dear grand children but I do think of you & them with warm love. I am so glad that you & Puck are planning to settle in Pittsburgh & Louisa & I are all in for it, so are The Golden Glows & After Glows & Donald Allstons. Bo & Sissy are planning to go to the St. Cecilia. It is nice to see how happy they are. You heard all that dear Minnie Alston did for me! Sums Children stick by the family! Love & a ~~happy~~ New Year for you & Puck & the children & grandchildren
>
> Affec. Aunt Ellen ("Ellie")
