notes: "Typescript copy, seventh and final substantive page of the will of Ann Drayton Perry. Page numbered '#7' at upper right; '794' written in pencil at corner. Page concludes the disposition of the plantations and personalty in trust for Edward D. Perry's children, frees his future ifsue from claims by their husbands, devises the residue of the testator's real and personal estate to Edward D. Perry for life with remainder to his children, appoints Edward D. Perry sole Executor, and closes with the attestation clause signed and sealed at Charleston in the second day of November in the year of our Lord one thousand Eight Hundred and Thirty. Transcribed literally for archival fidelity."
