Middle row: a small portrait of a fair-haired child (faintly captioned, illegible); a small head-and-shoulders portrait of a young child (captioned 'Sam G F.S.'); a small head-and-shoulders portrait of a boy in a sailor-style outfit captioned '\"Sam\" 14 years old — son of Samuel G F.S.'; a snapshot view of a clapboard plantation-style house with a wide porch ('Mt Hope'); and a mounted handwritten note on a folded sheet at right ('Letter from Ellie's Dad after [hearing of?] his [illegible] / Letter from his last [illness?] until he [died] when died'). Bottom row: a snapshot of a porch with seated figures (captioned 'Pictures — Bro Sam — Annie — Father'); a large interior snapshot of a study or library showing a moustachioed older man and a younger seated man at a writing-desk amid books, globes, and floral arrangements; a small newspaper obituary fragment headed 'S. G. FitzSimons'; and a landscape snapshot of trees with a clearing at right ('View of place from Mt. Hope'). Many of the cursive captions are pale and several remain uncertain."
