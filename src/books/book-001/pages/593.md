  A single newspaper obituary clipping for Oswald Beverly 'Bo' McEwan, 81, of Orlando, a former president of the Florida Bar Association. The clipping is in two pieces with the lower portion separated by a horizontal cut.

  **Oswald Beverly "Bo" McEwan** (b. c. 1909/10, d. 1990s) was the compiler's son-in-law: he married Amy and J. P. Walker's daughter **Mary Ann Walker** on 14 May 1941 at the Church of the Good Shepherd, Jacksonville (book-003 pp. 1–2). A WWII U.S. Army officer turned Orlando attorney, he later served as president of the Florida Bar and as U.S. Referee in Bankruptcy. He is the grandfather of Nancy Fletcher McEwan (b. 1946; mentioned on pages 600 and 601) and of James Walker McEwan, and the great-grandfather of the present custodian of this album.

> **Oswald Beverly McEwan**
>
> The service for Oswald Beverly "Bo" McEwan, 81, of Orlando, who died Wednesday, will be at 3 p.m. Saturday at The Cathedral of St. Luke in Orlando, with burial at Greenwood Cemetery in Orlando. Memorial contributions may be made to The Cathedral Church of St. Luke, 130 North Magnolia Avenue, Orlando FL 32801 or Hospice of Central Florida, 2500 Maitland Center Parkway, Suite 300, Maitland FL 32751.
>
> He was a former president of the Florida Bar Association, United States Referee in Bankruptcy and was on the Florida Real Estate Commission Trustee Attorney's Title Insurance Fund.
>
