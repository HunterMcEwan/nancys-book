Then I have a little [phrase] book in which a "Standard" Child is made to ask & answer the most wonderful questions to her Pa — And then there is a Dictionnaire which is really a Friend in Need when the boy [Quae] begins, with [Bridge] as he [skylined]. After that I come back to my proper [tongue] and express my [discontent] [or perhaps] [the angel] of [Faith] & Hell by reading a sketch of the Italian Poets by [Leigh Hunt] — I have only begun them — so that I have not got beyond that good little [Simonides].
