## Close of obituary (upper portion)

> reflected back in sympathy upon
> their lives — T. G. B. —

## Opening of a separate handwritten letter (lower portion)

> "I cannot tell you how shocked and
> grieved I was to hear of the death
> of poor Kit FitzSimons. I was very
> fond of him and from the bottom
> of my heart I sympathize with
> his widow and his ^his^ orphans. God help —
> "Weep not the dead — 'tis they alone
