A composite album page with four items.

**Upper left — folded paper note:**

A triangular fold of lined notebook paper inscribed in pencil. On the outward-facing face, in a child's hand:

> I love you
>
> from Greg [uncertain] who
>
> in these [uncertain] places

A second pencil inscription is visible on a triangular fold below:

> To
>
> Mother

*[A child's note folded into a hand-made envelope. See page 436 for the inside, inscribed "you are the best mother in the world".]*

**Upper right — newspaper clipping, *Sentinel Star*, Orlando, Friday, February 23, 1979 (page 12-D):**

> Pan American Bank of Orlando has named three [local] men and women to its board of directors and promoted four executives to vice president. New board members include Grace C. Lindblom, Ray Parkins and Fred McLeod.
>
> Lindblom has served as business advisor and managed investments for professional people in the area since 1956. Parkins is president of The Parkins Companies (investment securities), and McLeod is president of Plywoild Inc. and McLeod Corp.
>
> The new vice presidents include Andrea Akerlands, secretary to the president and board of directors; Christopher D. Morgan, marketing officer; **James W. McEwan**, commercial loan officer, and Helen M. Beck, trust officer.

*[The name "James W. McEwan" is underlined in pencil.]*

**Lower left — addressed envelope:**

A cream envelope with no postmark visible. In a blue-ink hand at the upper left, the return address:

> Box 329
> Columbia Falls,
> Mt. 59912

Addressed in the center:

> Mrs. Mary Ann
> 1129 South O[sceola]
> Orlando, Flor[ida]

*[Return address corresponds to Christopher G. McEwan, who lived in Columbia Falls, Montana — see page 414.]*

**Lower right — printed cream folder:**

A printed cream-colored folder bearing at the upper center the University of Central Florida Pegasus seal in brown:

> UNIVERSITY OF CENTRAL FLORIDA
> 1963

Below, in script:

> *University of*
> *Central Florida*

*[The cover of the commencement announcement; the inside is on page 437.]*
