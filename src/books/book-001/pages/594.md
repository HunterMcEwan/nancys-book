notes: |
  A loose title page from the 1940 (tenth) edition of "Who's Who in
  Railroading in North America," published by The Simmons-Boardman
  Publishing Corporation, 30 Church Street, New York. The page is
  foxed and spotted; a horizontal fold-crease runs across the lower
  third where the sheet was once folded in half (the crease is most
  visible as a slight line of soiling running between the title
  block and the publisher's imprint). There are no hidden pencil
  notes, captions, or attribution marks anywhere on the page —
  neither in the wide margins nor at the page edges nor faintly
  under the foxing. The page is a stand-alone keepsake. The
  accompanying biographical entry (if any, presumably for a Walker
  / FitzSimons / McEwan family member listed in the directory's
  main alphabetical section) is not present in the album; only the
  title page was saved. The volume's predecessor title — "The
  Biographical Directory of Railway Officials of America" — is
  given on the page itself, dating the series' rename to the 1930
  (sixth?) edition.
