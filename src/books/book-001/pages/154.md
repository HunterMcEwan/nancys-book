## Left column

> persons what a perfect type of the end of such a life — the service, & the time of day, & the full church, with the hush over it, that is not always then when the congregation is a large one, at a funeral — We went to Magnolia, & all the time we were there a bird was singing near us, the soft light of afternoon sunset, restoring on every thing, affected every one unconsciously, & instead of the shudder of parting with that part of Mother, I found myself realizing for the first time in my life entirely that *she* was not there, & the thought "take Holy Earth all that my soul holds dear" — I do not mean to tell you that such a feeling remained, or came before, but I had a feeling
