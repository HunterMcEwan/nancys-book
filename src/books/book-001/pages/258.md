Brig, criticism of someone's social conduct ('I think his nose is somewhat the worse'), Bro. Brig planning to spend his leisure in fixing up Mr. Pa's [property?] at Cypress [Pond?], anxiety about big neighbours, and indignation meetings against [the?] friend who cannot help sympathising. The right page introduces Mrs. Rhea (married to her Sosey), Mrs. Heyward's wedding details, a bridal trip, Claudian going up to Greenville, and notes on Mrs. C. and Mrs. Bellinger remaining in Stateburg."
