A short handwritten note on personal letterhead reading **Mrs. Charles Franklin Martin / 3219 Heyward Street / Columbia, South Carolina**. Written in blue ink, complete on this single sheet.

> Dear Amy, I found a copy of the story! It reads better from the printed page, although I have rewritten & improved it slightly. Hope you enjoy it — I am enclosing the new final paragraph which will interest you.
>
> Best wishes — Margaret M.
