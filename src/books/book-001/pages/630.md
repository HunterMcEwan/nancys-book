Two pages of handwritten genealogical notes in faded ink, recording marriages and children.

**Left-hand page:**

> **Julia Virginia Besselieu** married at Pottersville, S.C., by the Rev. M. Brucker to D. F. \[B. F.] Dunkin Perry.
>
> Their children are *[one fold/illegible word]*
>
> **Mary Annie**, married S. G. FitzSimons.
>
> **Elizabeth Fennell** — \[married] Wm. Lee Effin.
>
> **James O'Bannon** — killed in C.B. accident.
>
> **Dunkin Drayton** — unmarried.
>
> **Maurice Jane Mueller** — unmarried.
>
> **Rosa Allmuck** — married, **Andrew Mountford**.

**Right-hand page:**

> **Mary Annie Perry** married at Columbus, Georgia, in Church of S. and S. Jude in the morning of the 13th of April 1887 by the Rev. M. Brentee to **Samuel G. FitzSimons**.
>
> Their children:
>
> **Mary Perry** — born 30th \[May?] 1888. Married **James Vinton Walker**.
>
> **Theodore Drucker** — born 1st July 1890.
>
> **Dunkin Perry** — born 3rd of May 1893; died 16th February 1895.
>
> **Thomas Drucker** — born 3rd of November 1894; died 1st of May 1896.
>
> **Samuel Eckel** — born August 30th 1897; died July 21st 1898.
>
> **Mary Anne** — born September 30th 1899.
>
> **Samuel Gaillard** — born September 2nd 1904.
