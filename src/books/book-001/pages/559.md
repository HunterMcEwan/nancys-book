**Lower-left clipping (partial):**

> We have long loved Frank L. FitzSimons and Robert Lee Whitmire, among the tops of Henderson County speakers, gentlemen had the privilege of hearing both of them in Hendersonville during the past week … Both were veterans of World War I and both were at home in their roles as historians delivering an American Day commission and the talkfest the address as a memorial to those who gave their lives in the last war, the bodies of some of whom are now being returned. Mr. FitzSimons had a Generation award by safeguarding the Cradle of Hendersonville's Confederate Veterans, Captain Vance Christ, the orphanage of which by clip would be told being … [text continues, increasingly illegible].
