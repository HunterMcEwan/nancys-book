An album page with a long inscribed envelope at the top, a sepia oval studio portrait at the left, and a small addressed envelope at the lower left.

**Upper envelope** (blue cursive listing the enclosed certificates):

> Birth certificate — Amy Perry FitzSimons
>
> Certification of Marriage of Amy FitzSimons and J. P. Walker
>
> Death certificate — J. P. Walker

**Oval portrait:** Sepia studio photograph, oval format, of a dark-haired young woman in a high-necked dark dress, seated, holding a smiling fair-haired infant on her lap. The portrait is unidentified on the page itself, but its placement immediately above the envelope of certificates for Amy FitzSimons / J. P. Walker and on the same sheet as her son's birth-or-marriage documentation suggests this is **Amy FitzSimons (Mrs. James Pickens Walker)** with one of her children.

**Lower envelope** (addressed in brown cursive):

> Mr. & Mrs. J. P. Walker
>
> 3698 Hedrick St.
>
> Jacksonville
>
> Florida

*[The street number "3698" on this envelope differs by one digit from the "3648 Hedrick St." address on the envelope mounted at page 318; both forms appear in the album.]*
