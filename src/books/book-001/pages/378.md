An album leaf with a long inscribed envelope at the upper center, a sepia oval studio portrait at the left, and a small addressed envelope at the lower left. Right two-thirds of the leaf is blank.

**Upper envelope** (blue cursive, listing the enclosed certificates):

> Birth certificate — Amy Perry FitzSimons
>
> Certification of Marriage of Amy FitzSimons and J. P. Walker
>
> Death certificate — J. P. Walker

**Oval portrait** (sepia studio photograph in worn oval mat, the upper-left corner torn): A dark-haired young woman in a high-necked dark dress is seated, smiling faintly down, holding a fair-haired infant in a white smock on her lap. The infant grins broadly toward the camera. The photograph is unidentified on the page itself, but its placement immediately beneath the envelope cataloguing the Amy FitzSimons / J. P. Walker family papers strongly indicates **Amy FitzSimons (Mrs. James Pickens Walker)** with one of her children — either her firstborn daughter Amy Perry 'Buzzie' Walker (b. 20 March 1910, d. c. May 1911) or her elder son James Pickens Walker Jr. (b. 5 January 1912) as a toddler.

**Lower envelope** (addressed in brown cursive, no return address visible on the front):

> Mr. & Mrs. J. P. Walker
>
> 3698 Hedrick St.
>
> Jacksonville
>
> Florida
