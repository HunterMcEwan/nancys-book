A handwritten genealogical worksheet in blue ink. The compiler began writing the children's section, caught a slip (mis-pairing Amy's name with Henry's birthdate), and restated the entry below.

**First attempt (upper half):**

> Mary Ann FitzSimons — B. Sept. 30th 1899
> —M— Nov. 3rd 1920 —
> Donald McKay Allston — Born Aug. 9th 1884

> 1) Amy Perry Allston — B. May 6th 1948 *[the date belongs to her son; the compiler crossed through and restated below]*
> —M—
> Henry Burnett Fishburne — *[struck through]*

**Restated below:**

> Mary Ann FitzSimons — B. Sept. 30th 1899
> —M— Nov. 3rd 1920
> Donald McKay Allston — Aug. 9 1884

> 1) Amy Perry Allston — Born Aug. 22nd 1921
> —M— May 16th 1946
> Henry Burnett Fishburne —
>     1) Henry Burnett Fishburne II — Born May 6th 1948
>     2) Donald Allston Fishburne — Born Aug. 1st 1951

A pencilled note at the lower right edge:

> over —

*[The worksheet continues on page 397 (its verso), where the compiler enumerates the next two Allston children — Amarinthia Theodora and Donald McKay Jr. The Fishburne family is treated more fully on pages 350–353, including the wedding clipping for Amy Perry Allston's 1946 marriage to Henry Burnett Fishburne at Willtown Bluff plantation.]*
