A column clipping headed:

> **...Dr. Allison**
>
> Continued From Page 1-A

> The coadjutor-elect was ordained a priest in May 1953. He was the assistant rector of Columbia's Trinity Episcopal Church and a chaplain at the University of South Carolina from 1952 to 1954. He was an associate professor of church history at the University of the South from 1956 until 1967, and a professor of church history at Virginia Seminary from 1967 to 1975.
>
> Dr. Allison has been the rector of Grace Episcopal Church in Manhattan since 1975.
>
> Dr. Allison is married to the former Martha Allston Parker and has four children, ranging in age from 13 to 27.
>
> Consecration of the new bishop coadjutor is scheduled for Sept. 25, the eve of the regular convention of the Diocese of South Carolina.
