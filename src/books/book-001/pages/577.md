> W. A. LIVINGSTON, INC.
> WHOLESALE GROCERIES
>
> ORANGEBURG, S.C.
>
> March, 9th, 57.
>
> SINCERE CONGRATULATIONS TO BOTH OF
> YOU GENTLEMEN FOR YOUR PROMOTION AND
> WELL DESERVED RECOGNITION.
>
> CORDIALLY,
>
> W. A. LIVINGSTON.
>
> *[signed in blue ink]* W. A. Livingston

*[the cursive signature reads W. A. Livingston (matching both the engraved letterhead and the typed cue line) — not "J. A. Livingston". The opening flourish on the W is an ornate calligraphic loop, easily misread at low resolution but unambiguous on the high-resolution scan.]*
