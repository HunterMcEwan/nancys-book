> but he did not know whether they were going to Charleston or only go for as Rockland. As you know Frank is able to go that he will be with you on [Sunday?] but the [day?]. — Glory me how to John and tell him it is just so dull here as when he left and that if he is having as good or better time in Charleston, he has [better] stay until [the next?] with you. — After a week of [clouds] and [rain] weather, yesterday and today are lovely. With love for [Rosa?].
>
> Affectionately
>
> S.P.S.
>
> Remembrance for Julia.

*[Postscript at lower portion of the sheet:]*

> I have not heard from Louisa since you received your Doctor's degree. I know that it is happiness to you. — Tell John I have not been to her apartment since [I left].

*[Compiler's pencil note at the lower centre of the sheet:]*

> Uncle Sate & Ellie
