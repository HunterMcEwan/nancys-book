FitzSimons, the Charleston Library Society librarian. The writer signs herself 'Ellen' on the closing leaf (page 526) and is the same niece who writes to 'Aunt Ellen' on pages 527 and 530–531 — i.e., Ellen Jr. The writer hopes the letter will reach Aunt Ellen on Easter Sunday and apologizes for not having written sooner after the death of Theodore Barker."
