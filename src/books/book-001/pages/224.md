A handwritten letter page in cursive, light/black ink. The upper portion is a transcribed tombstone inscription, followed by a query about the children. The lower half of the page is blank.

> Don't [smell?] if I enclosed this it was on tomb stone too
>
> To the memory of a tender parent
> Mrs Susanna Milliken — she was
> born at Belfast Ireland and died
> 21 August 1808 in the 48th year of her
> age and of an affectionate Mother
> Edward Milliken a native of Belfast
> Ireland, who died 9th June 1808 in
> the 28 year of his age.
>
> Where did the seven sons come in?
> [John?] isn't mentioned on tomb stone
> There were evidently two Edwards
> Edward & Edward Patrick, William
> Hunter, Mathew Irvine, & Adam Tims
