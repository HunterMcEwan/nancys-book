  Typed legal indenture, page 1 of 2 (continues on 242; verso/Clerk-of-Court
  form is on 243; the back/show-through with a docketed "Copy" endorsement is
  on 245). Recites that on the 31st of January 1918 W. Huger FitzSimons and
  William R.W. Howe, qualified Executors of the Last Will and Testament of
  the late Theodore Gaillard Barker, deceased (the "Tody" Barker of
  Charleston who died at Pisgah Forest), of Charleston, SC, conveyed two
  tracts in Henderson County, NC to Robert E. Stepp for $2,000. The body
  sets out the metes-and-bounds description of the first parcel, beginning
  at a point in the North margin of FitzSimons Street (the street takes its
  name from the Barker–FitzSimons summer-place lands at Pisgah Forest) and
  proceeding by courses and distances. The first tract abuts the Southern
  Railway Company's right-of-way and W.C. Jordan's land, then runs along
  the South margin of Brookland Avenue and adjoins land of Thomas Jones.
  Continues on the next scan.

## Indenture (typed legal document, page 1)

> STATE OF NORTH CAROLINA
>
> COUNTY OF HENDERSON.
>
> THIS INDENTURE made this thirty first day of January in the year of our Lord, One thousand Nine hundred and Eighteen, between W. Huger FitzSimons and William R.W. Howe, qualified Executors of the Last Will and Testament of the late Theodore G. Barker, deceased, parties of the first part, and ROBERT E. STEPP, of the County of Henderson, North Carolina, party of the second part:
>
> WHEREAS the late Major Theodore G. Barker departed this life on or about the 31st day of Jan'y 1917 seized and possessed of the tracts of land hereinafter described and leaving in full force his Last will and testament bearing date the 11th day of May 1910 and duly admitted to probate in the office of the Clerk of Court of Henderson County, North Carolina, in and by which the said Theodore G. Barker empowered the persons who qualified as Executors upon said will to sell at public or private sale at their discretion at such time or times places or places in such parts or part, form and manner as they may determine, all or any part of his estate real and personal and to make execute and deliver to the purchaser or purchasers thereof complete title to the same, as will more fully appear by reference to said will:
>
> AND WHEREAS W. Huger FitzSimons and William R.W. Howe have qualified as executors upon said will and execute this deed pursuant to the power therein contained:
>
> NOW THEREFORE THIS INDENTURE WITNESSETH, that the said parties of the first part, for and in consideration of the sum of Two thousand ($2000.00) dollars and other valuable consideration in hand paid by the said party of the second part, the receipt whereof is hereby acknowledged, have bargained and sold, conveyed and confirmed, and by these presents do bargain and sell and convey and confirm unto the said party of the second part his heirs and assigns forever all that following described pieces and parcels of land lying and being in the County of Henderson and State of North Carolina, and known and designated as follows, to-wit:—
>
