**Right column — Emma Dee Walker Corbell:**

> Emma Dee Walker —
> Born — Oct. 18th — 1915

> —M— Oct. 9th 1937
>
> Dr. Robert Lawrence Corbell Jr. — *[ditto Oct. 9th 1937]*

A pencilled note at the lower right:

> over —

*[The worksheet captures only the elder two of Amy and J. P. Walker Sr.'s four children — James Pickens Walker Jr. (1912–1969) and Emma Dee Walker Corbell (1915–1959) — implying that the verso ('over —') continues with the younger two: Mary Ann Walker McEwan (1918–1975) and Amy Perry Walker (b. 1921). On the densely annotated family page 392, J. P. Walker Jr.'s death date is given as Jan. 21, 1969; this worksheet's '2' may be a slip for '21'. The Corbell line is detailed further on page 393, where Dee Corbell's 1972 marriage in Corbeil-Essonnes, France, to Claude Aimé-Gramond and the births of their sons Eric (1976) and Nicolas (1978) are added in late-1970s pencil.]*
