> # Recognition and Investiture of a Diocesan Bishop
>
> *The Congregation remains seated while the lay officials and clergy of the Diocese are escorted to their places.*
>
> *When the ministers and people have assembled in the Cathedral Church, the Chancel Party, including the Presiding Bishop, the Retiring Bishop, and the other Bishops, is escorted from the South Door to the Chancel.*
>
> *The welcoming procession moves through the congregation to the West Door.*
>
> *The new Bishop, standing outside the door, knocks upon it three times.*
>
> *The President of the Standing Committee, serving as Warden, opens the door.*
>
> *The new Bishop says*
>
> Open for me the gates of righteousness; I will enter them
> and give thanks to the Lord.
>
> *Warden*   The Lord prosper you;
> we wish you well in the Name of the Lord.
