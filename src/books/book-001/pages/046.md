## Bottom-of-leaf handwritten text now visible

Below the lowest envelope and previously hidden, in cursive ink:

> The following inscription is on the back stone of Abigail Barlow

> wife of Richard R. Barlow
>
> Consort of Wisdom & a Smile to every grief and sister of any kind dweller of this life. She was born June 28th, July 12th 1749 & died of paralysis on Sunday 5 of Dec. 1791.
>
> Her bosom was [uncertain] in society anny, no [illegible], [uncertain], and a friend.
>
> A trial of this be [uncertain] this is true."
