A single sheet of cream paper in blue cursive, heavily revised and overwritten. Dated only "Sunday."

> Dear Minnie —                      Sunday —
>
> I feel ashamed when I don't write you more often. Where time goes, I don't know. Sam, Janice & Bo were here a week with us, & it was wonderful. Little Bo is precious & ~~the only~~ word he says is "cooky," & when your hand goes up for one or the other I missed them so badly when they left. Am sending you the letter I received from Sam Fri., anticipating this but he told ~~Jed~~ not to tell me until he made his decision. I didn't sleep very good Fri. night — wondering if Sam is doing the right thing, but ~~&~~ I know he is well-headed & may make a success. It is a heating & air-conditioning business. They found out Mr. Cooperath has a heart murmur & I think he'd like to retire. It will really be wonderful to have *[continues — verso/next page not present in scan]*
