The visible main horizontal text recounts the writer being asked to convey the news of the death of a 'dear Sister'; mentions a visit to Aunt Kate; describes Mrs. Walker and Christie nursing her at the Island on the morning of July 29; says she had not been well for some weeks before, suffering from severe nervous headaches, and seemed depressed and even spoke doubtfully of her visit to Aunt Kate. Many words remain illegible. Likely a continuation/portion of the multi-page letter that begins on page 254 or a related letter inserted into the same album sequence."
