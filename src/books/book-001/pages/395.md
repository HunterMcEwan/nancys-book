> **MARY HADDON**

Three numbered children:

> 1) Samuel Gaillard **III**
> —M—
> Janice Louise Coonradt — March 19th 1960
> *[red ink: "date" / "any issue?"]*
>
> 2) Mary Ann — *[red ink: "date"]*
> —M— April 29th 1957
> Clarence Edwin McLane
>     Dunbrie — B. Dec. 1st 1958
>
> 3) Grace — B. Dec. 26th *[red ink: "date" / "any issue?"]*

The lower half of the sheet is blank apart from faint mirror-image show-through from the verso (the Walker–Corbell worksheet, page 398).

*[Samuel Gaillard FitzSimons Jr. (1904–1961) was the compiler Amy FitzSimons Walker's youngest brother — the seventh child of Samuel Gaillard FitzSimons Sr. and Mary Ann Perry, per the family page 339. His three children with Mary Haddon are tabulated more completely on p339.]*
