A clipped newspaper column with a photograph of Allison at top, captioned beneath.

> **Dr. C.F. Allison**
>
> **Bishop Coadjutor Consecration Ceremony Today**
>
> The Rev. Dr. C. FitzSimons Allison will be consecrated as bishop coadjutor of the Episcopal Diocese of South Carolina today at 11 a.m. at Gaillard Municipal Auditorium.
>
> Dr. Allison will become bishop of the diocese upon the retirement of the current bishop, the Rt. Rev. Gray Temple.
>
> The Rt. Rev. John M. Allin, presiding bishop of the 3 million-member Episcopal Church in the United States, will be the chief consecrator at the service. About 30 bishops of the Episcopal Church are expected to attend the ceremony.
>
> The service will be open to the public. Church officials are urging interested persons to arrive at least a half hour before the start of the service. Free parking will be available at the auditorium.
>
> Dr. Allison, a native of Columbia, has been rector of Grace Episcopal Church in New York for five years.
>
> The Episcopal Diocese of South Carolina has about 26,000 members and 68 parishes or mission churches, of which more than a dozen are in the Charleston area.
