notes: "Printed solicitation flyer from the Barbour County Historical Society of Philippi, W. Va. (zip 26416) inviting residents to submit family stories for inclusion in 'Leave Your Thumbprint in Time,' a Barbour County history book. The flyer is illustrated with the society's seal at upper left and a fingerprint motif at upper right. Members of the book and family chairman committee are listed at left in a single column with telephone numbers (area code 304). Deadline March 30, 1979. Signed by Wilbert Tenney and Mary S. Coffman. Included with the album because the compiler's sister-in-law Anna Walker Modisette (Amy's husband J. P. Walker Sr.'s sister) was preparing the Walker family typescripts on pp660-664 for submission to this Barbour County book."

> Barbour County History Book
> Philippi, W. Va. 26416
>
> **Leave Your Thumbprint in Time**
>
> **IF YOU DON'T, WHO WILL?**
>
> 100 years from today, what will people know about you? Probably little, unless you write it down! And unless that writing is preserved in a book, that too may be lost!
>
> **HERE'S THE SOLUTION: WRITE IT DOWN! THE STORY OF YOUR FAMILY HOUSEHOLD!**
> It's simple. Send us your story of 500 words or less and one picture (if possible). Your story must be typewritten, double spaced. You may send more than one picture, these will be used on a space available basis. Please indicate first, second, and third choice in case all of them cannot be used. We make every effort to return photographs, but due to the number of times they will be handled, it would be best to send copies of any valuable or irreplaceable photos.
>
> **THERE IS NO FEE FOR HAVING YOUR STORY (500 words) AND ONE PICTURE PUBLISHED, AND YOU ARE NOT REQUIRED TO BUY A BOOK!**
>
> If you are living in, or have previously lived in Barbour County, we want your Family Household History. In writing your story, be sure to include the area in which you live or lived. Write about special family things, include pioneer tales, and current activities. (refer to the sample stories on the reverse side of this page). Write your story as if telling future generations about your family and your ancestors.
>
> In addition to family stories, we will be gathering stories and pictures about Barbour County schools, churches, organizations, businesses and others.
>
> **WE MUST HAVE YOUR STORY AND PICTURES BY MARCH 30, 1979, SO DON'T DELAY!**
>
> Send to: Barbour County History Book
> Philippi, W. Va. 26416
>
> If you have any questions, please contact any of the book committee members listed at left.
>
> Sincerely Yours,
>
> Wilbert Tenney &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Mary S. Coffman
