*[The block records descendants of Gaillard Stoney FitzSimons (b. Oct. 1864) and Susan Lockwood (b. 1870, d. Sept. 9, 1909) through their three children — Frank Lockwood, Ellen Milliken (II), and Katharine Gaillard FitzSimons — and on into a third and fourth generation. Frank Lockwood F.S. married Margarita Kershaw (b. Oct. 3); their son Frank Lockwood Jr. married Clara Mather FitzSimons (b. March 26, 1926) and had Frank Lockwood 3rd (b. Aug. 8, 1950) and Samuel Dunkin (b. Sept. 18, 1952). Katharine Gaillard married Charles Hardy Waterfall (1965) and had Gaillard FitzSimons Waterfall and George Howard. Margarita Consuelo FitzSimons (b. Nov. 21) married Donald McKay Allston Jr. (b. Feb. 20, 1925) and had four children, two of whom — Margarita Consuelo and Mary Ann — both died Aug. 27, 1962 (the page does not preserve the cause).]*
