*[Editorial note: the sheet conflates two generations of Perrys. **Edward Perry the Younger** (Gen 6 on this chart; testator of the 1797 will on p. 609, d. 2 June 1800 age 26) was married to **Ann Drayton** — not to Maria Ann Mauger. His widow Ann Drayton Perry is the 1830 testator of the will on pp. 612–618; that will explicitly names Maria Ann Mauger as the "intended wife" of their son **Edward D. Perry** (the next generation down, B. F. D. Perry's father). The chart's entry of Maria Ann Mauger as Gen-6 wife therefore belongs one generation later. The Generation 5 wife's full given name resolves on enhancement to **Julia Virginia** Bessellieu (her death is recorded under "Julia Virginia Perry" in the church records). The compiler **Amy FitzSimons** (Mrs. James Pickens Walker) is Generation 3; her daughter Mary Ann Walker McEwan is Generation 2; granddaughter Nancy Fletcher McEwan is Generation 1. The sheet was prepared no earlier than the 3 Feb 1968 marriage and no later than 1969 (Mary Ann Walker still recorded as "Living 1969"). It was filed as part of Nancy's own Colonial Dames application (C.D. Papers #220 in Mrs. O. B. McEwan's name).]*
