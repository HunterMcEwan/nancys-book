A single black-ink drawing centered on an otherwise blank album page. At the top, a small boar stands passant on a wreath atop the helm. Below, dark mantling curls outward to either side around a serrated-edge shield charged with two crescents in chief, a fesse across the middle, and a single crescent in base. An empty ribbon-like banderole curves across the foot of the shield. A small artist's monogram (illegible) appears at the lower right of the banderole.

Beneath the shield, in blackletter:

> **Fitz Simons**
