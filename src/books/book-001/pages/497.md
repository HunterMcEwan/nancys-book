> 4 Savage St
> Charleston
> S.C.
>
> Dec. 9, 1948
>
> Dear Amy:
>
> That was a lovely letter that you wrote me and I especially liked what you said about your dear father.
>
> You and Minnie have always seemed like Louisa's sisters & her happiest days were spent on the Plantation with you.
>
> I am glad that Anne & Pickens have enjoyed the contact with Sam Stoney. He is gifted in his speech & me & his pleasure in writing of them. Sam is all right when he is not trying to prove that his Singleton ancestors who *"intermarried with Africans"* did what others *(Stoneys & Fitz Simons etc.)* did! He wanted me say that my father changed the name of his Back River plantation to *"Blue Resin"* that was the name of the California Stoneys' (cousin Tom Stoney's) place.
