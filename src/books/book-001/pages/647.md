A loose sheet mounted to the album page, filled edge-to-edge with a hand-drawn pencil descendant chart connecting the Perry, FitzSimons, Walker, and McEwan branches.

**Upper-right marginal note (in pencil, in the compiler's hand):**

> the 3rd m[arried] — Col. Wm.
> Fuller of Beaufort being
> the 3rd husband.

**Top row (Perry generation — matriarch with three husbands):**

> [Edw'd?] Perry — *m.* ~~Harriet~~ Maria Ann Mauger (*née* Hedley)

*[The first name of "Perry" is clipped by the trimmed left edge of the loose sheet; only "—rd Perry" is visible, suggesting an abbreviated "Edwd." (Edward) or "Richd." (Richard). The strike-through "Harriet" is the compiler's own self-correction to "Maria Ann"; her third husband, per the upper-right note, was Col. Wm. Fuller of Beaufort.]*

**Below — children of Maria Ann Hedley + her Perry husband:**

> Benjamin Dunkin Perry — *m.* Julia ~~Dessallieu~~ Bezalieu *[orange highlighter slash through the struck word]*
> Roda — *m.* Andrew Crawford *[orange highlighter slash]*

*[Two near-identical French-looking surnames appear stacked, the lower one struck through; the compiler may have been uncertain about the spelling of Julia's surname.]*

**Next row (FitzSimons generation):**

> Mary Anne *[Perry]* — B. July 14th 1859
> Gaillard
> *m.* Samuel FitzSimons — B. Jan 8th 1854 D. Mar 16 1930
> B. Dec. 29th 1826 D. Oct 16th 1930
>
> *[siblings / collateral relatives written across the row:]*
> Elizabeth Howell
> William Coffin
> Dunkin Drayton — Jane Miller

*[The dual sets of birth/death dates apply to the two FitzSimons figures listed — Samuel Gaillard FitzSimons Sr. (b. 28 Dec 1856) and his wife Mary Anne Perry "Minnie" (d. Jan 1934). Some dates on the chart are at odds with other sources in the album; the chart should be cross-checked against pp271 / 629 / 463.]*

**Third row (the seven FitzSimons siblings — children of Samuel Gaillard Sr. + Mary Anne Perry):**

> Amy Perry — *m.* James P. Walker
> Theodore Barker — *m.* (Clara Mather)
> Minnie *[= Mary Annie FitzSimons]* — *m.* (Donald Allston)
> Samuel Gaillard *[Jr.]* — *m.* Mary Hodge

*[Spouses written on the line below — Clara Mather (i.e. Clara Hamilton Mueller, T. B. FitzSimons's wife) and "Donald Allston" (Donald McKay Allston Sr., Mary Annie's husband).]*

**Fourth row (the great-grandchildren of Samuel Gaillard Sr.):**

> *[Theodore Barker FitzSimons's children:]*
