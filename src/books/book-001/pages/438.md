The inside of the engraved cream commencement announcement folder. Printed in brown script:

> The President, the Faculty
>
> and the Graduating Classes
>
> announce the
>
> Commencement Exercises
>
> of the
>
> University of Central Florida

Below in blue ballpoint, in a flowing modern hand:

> Nancy Fletcher McEwan
>
> May, 1986
