A single leaf of personal stationery, blue ballpoint cursive on cream paper. Letterhead printed in blue across the top center:

> **MRS. THEODORE B. FitzSIMONS**
> 184 TACOMA CIRCLE
> ASHEVILLE, NORTH CAROLINA

Body of the letter:

> Sunday.
>
> Dearest Amy,
>
> Thank you so much for the lovely card and very pretty bond. I do appreciate both.
>
> I hope you and Pucci had a happy Christmas — I have not heard if you went to Mary Ann's this year.
>
> Mother and I went to Mather's Christmas eve, returning yesterday, and of course had a very cheerful time (just full of cheer!). Went over to Margarita & Frank's for a drink Christmas eve — a nice open fire, everything very festive. I thought of you and Pucci, knowing you'd enjoy it. Kip, James and the boys were up for Christmas.
