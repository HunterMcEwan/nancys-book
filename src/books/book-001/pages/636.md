[…and came] to Charleston with his parents in his
infancy. The Rev. John Hedley was married
to Miss Sarah Russell in St. Mary's Church, London.
He was the son of Roger Hedley of Callowhill,
Northumberland — Eng.

**Deaths.**

The Rev. John Hedley D.D. about 68 yrs.
father of John L. Hedley, died on the 9th
of February 1832. Buried in St. Michael's
Church yard.

William Hedley, aged 28 yrs. 6 mo. & 28 days, brother
of J. L. Hedley died on the morning of the
19th of December 1832. Buried in St. Michael's
Church yard.

John Lucius Hedley died on the 3rd day of
January 1853, after having sustained three
strokes of paralysis at his residence on
15 King St., in the 55th year of his age.

> Eliza Steele, née Hedley, buried in St. Paul's
> Church yard.
>
> Maria Ann Fuller, née Hedley, buried
> in Church yard of Baptist Church in Barnwell, S.C.

*[Margin annotation in pencil, rotated, bracketing the last two entries: "Sisters of John Hedley."]*

*[Inserted by Minnie P. FitzSimons 1924.]*
