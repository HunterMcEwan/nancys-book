[…and came] to Charleston with his parents in his
infancy. The Rev. John Hadley was married
[to] Miss Sarah Russell in St. Mary's Church, London.
He was the son of Roger Hadley of Callowhill,
Northumberland — Eng.

**Deaths.**

The Rev. John Hadley D.D. about 68 yrs.
father of John L. Hadley, died on the 9th
of February 1832. Buried in St. Michael's
Church yard.

William Hadley, aged 28 yrs. 6 mo. & 28 days, brother
of J. L. Hadley died on the morning of the
19th of December 1832. Buried in St. Michael's
Church yard.

John Lucius Hadley died on the 3rd day of
January 1853, after having sustained three
strokes of paralysis at his residence on
15 King St. in the 55th year of his age.

Eliza Steele, née Hadley, buried in St. Paul's
Church yard.

Mary Ann Fuller, née Hadley, buried
in Church yard of Baptist Church in Denmark, S.C.

*[Inserted by Minnie P. FitzSimons 1924.]*
