A single printed and hand-colored heraldic illustration occupies the center of an otherwise blank album page. At the top, a small radiant sun with a face. Below the sun, an eagle stands on a wreath atop a tilted knight's helm with green and silver-white mantling spreading to either side. The shield beneath shows two crescents in chief, a silver fesse charged with a crescent across the middle, and a crescent in base, all argent on a sable field.

Beneath the shield, in blackletter:

> **Fitz Simons**
