> Spartanburg, S.C.
> June 16, 1940.
>
> Dear Amy,
>
> Because people are prone to be indulgent to white hair and are willing to accept it as an adequate excuse ~~for~~ almost any shortcoming, I am rapidly learning to use old age as an alibi for all my faults and failings.
