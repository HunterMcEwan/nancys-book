> Charleston S. C.
>
> April 23/1910
>
> Dear Cousin Min,
>
> I am sending you the Perry lines as nearly as I can make ~~them~~ out; that is those in which we are interested. I hope that it will be clear to you, and interesting.
>
> It has been fascinating work to me looking up these old dead and gone people. I always feel a certain kind of intimacy with ~~them~~, and as if I really knew old grandmother **Rosamond** and ~~the~~ others personally. Such different people they seemed to us and I envy them their quiet, placid lives, free from the bustle and hustle of these
>
