*[Christopher Gaillard McEwan — Amy FitzSimons's grandson via her daughter Mary Ann Walker McEwan — graduates from law school at age c. 25 on a Saturday evening in 1968. The middle name "Gaillard" carries forward the family's Lowcountry Huguenot line: Gaillard came into the FitzSimons inheritance through Christopher FitzSimons 5th's mother Ellen Milliken Barker (whose maternal ancestry was Gaillard), and was kept alive by the compiler as a given name across the next two generations (cf. Gaillard Stoney FitzSimons "Gaillie," Amy's father).]*
