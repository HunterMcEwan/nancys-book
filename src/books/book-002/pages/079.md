Top row — two snapshots:

- Left: a small child in a long white dress standing at the foot of a wooden porch staircase, holding the newel.
- Right: a small girl in white in a garden, with a seated woman in a long skirt beyond.

Captions beneath, in blue ink:

> Ilee — 132 Rutledge Ave.

> Ilee + Mother

Middle row — three smaller snapshots:

- Left: a Black woman in a white headscarf holding a fair-haired baby beside an older infant in a long gown, on a brick patio.
- Center: two fair-haired infants posed together on a step.
- Right: an infant standing in a wooden slatted crib indoors.

Captions beneath, in blue ink:

> Pickens + Ilee 132 Rutledge.

> Pickens —

At the bottom of the page, mounted across the full width — a worn cream envelope bearing a child's looping pencil scrawl of letters across its face. Inscription beneath in blue ink:

> My 1st letter from Pickens.
