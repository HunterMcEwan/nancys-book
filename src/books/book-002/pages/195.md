A large black-and-white studio portrait of a smiling infant seated and bringing one hand to his mouth, wearing a pale smocked dress.

Caption beneath in blue ink:

> Christopher Gaillard McEwan born
> June 27th 1942 in Orange General Hospital
> in Orlando, Fla.

*[the high-resolution scan resolves the first-line trailing word as "born" (the sentence continues onto the next line), correcting phase 1's misreading of a second name "Susan." Orange General Hospital, Orlando, was founded 1918 as a 50-bed community hospital and renamed Orange Memorial Hospital in 1946; it later became part of Orlando Health.]*
