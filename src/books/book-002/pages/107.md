A small cream envelope mounted on the page, with a 2-cent red Washington stamp at upper right. The circular postmark to the left of the stamp reads:

> YONGES ISLAND, S.C.
> MAY 27
> 4 30 PM
> 1926

Addressed in brown fountain-pen ink:

> Mrs. J. P. Walker
> 1535 — Post Street.
> Jacksonville
> Florida.
