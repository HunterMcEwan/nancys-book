notes: "Album page with three horizontal sepia snapshots stacked vertically. Each is captioned in blue ink. The pictures show children mounted on a small horse or donkey at the family farm — a man in a dark coat (apparently Puck, James Pickens Walker) and other children stand alongside. Identifications are by handwritten caption: 'Sis – Patch and Peach helping', 'Pickens and the Watt[s]', and 'Children ride Peggy — the donkey'. 'Patch' and 'Peach' (Patchy) are family nicknames; on page 025 Amy refers to her baby sister as 'Patchy'."

Three sepia snapshots stacked vertically, each captioned in blue ink above or below.

Caption above the top print:

> Sis – Patch and Peach helping

Top print — three small children seated together on a small horse on a sunlit lawn, a man in a dark coat steadying them from the right; a person in white stands beside a tree in the background.

Caption above the middle print:

> Pickens and the [Watt]

Middle print — a man in a dark coat at left and a man in lighter clothes at right lead a small donkey across an open field, two children seated on the donkey and two more small children walking alongside.

Caption above the bottom print:

> Children ride Peggy — the donkey —

Bottom print — four small children seated atop a donkey in front of a clapboard house, a man in dark clothes and a straw hat standing at the right holding the donkey's lead.
