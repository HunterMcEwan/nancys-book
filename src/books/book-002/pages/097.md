Top — three small sepia snapshots in a row:
- Left: a small boy in white shirt and shorts seen from behind on a lawn.
- Center: a small boy on a piazza wearing a Native American–style feathered headdress costume.
- Right: a small boy carried in the arms of a man in dark clothes on a porch.

Middle — two snapshots flanking a block of caption text:
- Left: a small boy seated on a tricycle on a brick walk beside a wooden stair railing.
- Right: a long horizontal print of a row of small children lined along a porch railing, several waving American flags.
