A single sheet of cream stationery, written on one side in brown fountain-pen ink:

> Dear little Aimie:
>
> Your boy has been
> such a pleasure to us all,
> and I hate to see him
> go. I hope he will always
> feel like coming to us. The
> little children all like him.
> Minnie has been going to Fair
> View for two days getting the
> house cleaned and ready
> to move into on Saturday.
> I pray it may be alright; I know
