Continuing in black ink:

> has been at home for some weeks now. Jessica has spent too much in our visit. [Minnie] — she did not accept the Hammonds' pay, a think of it. She was nice I think as she is independent as she now is. Minnie has kept a very poor cook, and a [mid-single] fool to run after the children. Min has [Loo Wengel] back and is as usual. Clara very busy but happy. I have Julia who is at her best.
>
> Love to Puck.
>
> Devotedly,
>
> Mother.

*[Lower half of the sheet blank.]*
