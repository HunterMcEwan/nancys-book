*[Page 4, marked '·4·' at the top:]*

> I hope the girls husbond's are alright and safe so they can return and live happy again. We havent heard from my son-in law in over a month. Miss Maryon say she could tell us about him. he landed in Italy last year this time from africa. he was promoeated the first of the year from Sgt. to Staff sgt. and is with the 25th Chemical Decon Co. I belive he must be with Genral Mark Clark's 5 tharmey in Itley. Tell miss maryon if she can tell us something abat it please do altho it wont help matters any I dont know if thay sent him to France or not. The toughest fighting is going on now. write me if you will mrs Walker.
>
> Respectfully, Edna.
