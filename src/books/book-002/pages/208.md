**Upper photograph** — a black-and-white snapshot, mounted on a white card, of a small girl in a pale dress and white shoes standing on a lawn in front of a low one-story house with a brick chimney. Captioned in blue ink below:

> The Corbett home in Mary View Hospital Portsmouth Va.
> November 23rd 1946.

*[The phrasing "home in Mary View Hospital" is as written; likely the family was living in housing associated with the hospital where the father was stationed or working.]*

**Lower envelope** — a small pale envelope with a circular Portsmouth, Va. postmark dated AUG 14 1957 and a postage meter or cancellation stripe at the right. A purple 3-cent Wildlife Conservation stamp is affixed at the upper right. Addressed in blue fountain-pen ink:

> Mrs. James Pickens Walker
> Route 2, Box 393
> Hendersonville,
> N. C.
