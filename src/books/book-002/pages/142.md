*[At upper right:]* Tuesday night —

*[Embossed circular monogram or crest at top center.]*

Mummie darling —

I'm hoping this'll get to you on your birthday, but — I'm not sure. The trains run on I've figured 'em out anyway. I'm missing the dearest mother in all the world a happy birthday. She is the dearest mother, too; no one ever could ever hope to be as wonderful as she is. I sure love her dearly. I cannot help loving her when she is so darling as she is. I want her to know that her daughter thinks of her gentle, gentle ways and misses her terribly, too. She'll be thinking of her very sweetest of them all Sunday, too — I wish I could be there to say happy birthday and give you a birthday kiss! But — as it is — I'll have to dream you one.

How the gleam getter there yet? You should have seen the read from Killie last night about 11:40 P.M. Someone walked in with an envelope in her hand — looking as if she had seen a ghost — and said *in*…
