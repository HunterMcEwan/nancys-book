notes: "A single-sheet letter on thin cream paper, written in faded blue ink in a flowing cursive that is partly indistinct against the back-bleed of writing on the verso. Headed only 'Saturday' at the upper right. Opens 'Dearest light Brown' (possibly a family nickname). Wishes a belated Happy Birthday, encloses Valentines from June, and reports on a missed Sunday school meeting. Much of the lower half is hard to decipher because the page is heavily creased and the writer's pen has skipped."
