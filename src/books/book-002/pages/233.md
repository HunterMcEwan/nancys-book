On Camp Rockbrook stationery (red cardinal cresting):

> **ROCKBROOK**
>
> *Camp for Girls*
>
> **BREVARD, N.C.**

> Dear Grandmother,
>
> I am looking forward to come and visit you when I get out of camp. I am having a good time at camp. Last night we had some skits. Each cabin received a bag with a few little things in it. My made-like a dentist office. The counselors judged and we won second place.
>
> See you soon!
>
> Love,
>
> Nancy
>
> P.S. Phone me because I can not phone you. Visit me on a Saturday.
