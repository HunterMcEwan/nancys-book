Four photographs mounted on an album sheet, captioned in blue ink.

**Upper left** — a black-and-white snapshot of a small girl in a white bonnet, indoors.

> Nancy — Summerlin St.
> Orlando

*[Street name reads "Summerlin St." — the McEwans' first Orlando address before 1129 S. Osceola St. (cf. page 215, where baby Jim is also captioned at Summerlin St.). Summerlin is a historic Orlando thoroughfare bordering Thornton Park. Phase 1 read the cursive as "Sussmate"; on Phase 2 review the second word is clearly "Summerlin".]*

**Upper center** — a small snapshot of a child standing beside the trunk of a palm tree on a lawn.

> Chris (with palm) 5 years old [taken in front of] our house
> Orlando Fla

**Upper right** — a snapshot mounted on a white card showing a small child running across a lawn toward the camera, with a house and palms in the background.

*[The caption for this snapshot is contained within the "Chris (with palm)" annotation between the upper-center and upper-right prints.]*

**Lower right** — a tinted color studio portrait of a curly-haired girl with hazel eyes and a pink bow in her hair.

> Nancy Fletcher McEwan 19—
