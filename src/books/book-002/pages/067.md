> **WALKER.**—Died at Weldon, N. C., on the morning of June 30, 1911, **AMY FITZSIMONS**, infant daughter of Mr and Mrs J. P. Walker. Relatives and Friends are invited to attend Services and Interment at Magnolia Cemetery at 10 A. M., July 1, 1911.
