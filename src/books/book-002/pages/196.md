Oswald Beverley McEwan** (Mary Ann Walker's husband, baby Chris's father; US Army officer stationed in Louisiana/Texas in 1942), not Mary Ann's brother James Pickens Walker Jr. (also nicknamed 'Bo'). Both photos showing a uniformed soldier with the baby are Bo McEwan + Chris. Mary Ann and Bo McEwan married 14 May 1941; Chris (= Christopher Gaillard McEwan, Hunter McEwan's uncle — Hunter's father is Chris's younger brother James Walker 'Jim' McEwan) would have been ~8½ months old in these 1942 photos, putting his birth in spring 1942.
