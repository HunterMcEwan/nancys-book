A sepia-toned formal portrait photograph of a young man, head-and-shoulders, three-quarter view to the left, in a herringbone-pattern suit jacket, white shirt, and pale necktie. Hair neatly combed back.

Caption beneath the image in blue ink, in the compiler's hand:

> Pickens while at W. & L.

*[James Pickens Walker Jr., during his years (1929–1933) as an undergraduate at Washington and Lee University, Lexington, Virginia.]*
