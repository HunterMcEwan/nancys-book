A black-and-white photograph, uncaptioned. A two-story wood-frame house at center-left, partly obscured by the massive trunk and arching branches of a live oak draped in Spanish moss. A dark touring car is parked at the side of the house beside a flight of porch steps. Two chickens are pecking on the lawn in the right foreground; a small outbuilding stands at the far right edge.
