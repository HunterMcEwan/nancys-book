**Top photograph — caption beneath in blue ballpoint:**

> Dee & Robin Corbell on patio of HOME —

A black-and-white snapshot of a patio with metal-frame outdoor furniture — chairs, a chaise longue with striped cushion, and a small table — and two figures: a woman standing at center holding a small child aloft (the woman in glasses appears to be Emma Dee/"Ilee" with one of her children).

**Middle left photograph — caption beneath:**

> Chris McEwan + Missy — 1129 Osceola St.

A black-and-white snapshot of a boy in a horizontally striped shirt standing in a yard before a brick house, beside a tree, with a small dog ("Missy") at his feet.

**Middle right photograph — caption beneath:**

> Chris McEwan & his
> 1st wild turkey —

A black-and-white snapshot of a boy in a striped t-shirt holding a large turkey by its body, the bird's spread tail-feathers prominent in front of him.

**Bottom left photograph — caption beneath:**

> Dee Corbella. Portsmouth.
>
> Living room at 303 Norman Rd.

A black-and-white snapshot of a young girl in a short floral-print sundress standing in a furnished living room, with a side table, lamp, and other furniture visible around her.

**Bottom right photograph — caption beneath:**

> Puck Corbell on patio
> Ports mouth V[a].

A black-and-white snapshot of a boy reclining in a striped fabric hammock slung beneath a porch roof.

*[The "Corbell" surname is consistent throughout the compiler's captions on the surrounding pages (see also pages 195, 202). "Dee Corbella" is the compiler's casual feminine form for her granddaughter Dee, a sibling of the boy "Puck" Corbell pictured in the lower-right print. "Chris McEwan" is the compiler's grandson (b. 1942), eldest of Mary Ann Walker McEwan's children.]*
