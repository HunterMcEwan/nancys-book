**Top row** — a group of seven children in winter coats and hats posed outdoors.

Caption beneath, in pencil:

> Pickens + Ilee with the Waite children

**Middle row** — two snapshots side by side.

- *Left:* Two small children with an older child crossing a rustic footbridge over a stream.
- *Right:* Three children seated on a fallen log under live oaks.

Caption beneath the pair, in pencil:

> Pickens — Ilee + Mary Ann — and Amy Allston at Mount Hope and Fairview —

**Lower left** — a small girl in dark dress standing beside a brick column or wall.

Caption beneath, in pencil:

> Mary Ann — Clarksburg W. Va.

**Lower right** — a formal vignette studio portrait of a curly-haired child in a white dress.

Caption beneath, hand-lettered in pencil:

> MARY ANN (about 2)

*[The parenthetical "about 2" gives the sitter's age, dating the portrait c. 1920 (Mary Ann Walker b. 8 June 1918).]*
