Top — a horizontal landscape print showing a long low white-walled house under live oaks at the edge of a marsh or river. Caption beneath:

> Cain Island.

Middle row — three smaller prints:

- Left: a motor launch tied at a wooden dock. A handwritten caption to the left, partly cropped, reads:

  > [The Launch]

- Center: figures crouched in tall marsh grass with a flock of ducks or geese.
- Right: a man in a dark hat and white shirt holding up a large fish on a stringer.

Caption to the right of the man with the fish, in blue ink:

> Marie Petite +
> Charles —

Beneath the central marsh photograph, a caption in blue ink:

> Marie [illegible] →
> [illegible] →

Bottom row — at left, a small child in white standing in tall grass at the water's edge; at right, a horse- or mule-drawn cart with a single driver on a sandy road.
