My best love to both of [you] and to the children when you see them and when you write —

Kate sends her love — Please don't come to Wilmington without letting me know — I do want so much to see both of you from time to time —

> Affectionately,
>
> Julia

**Feb. 10, 1945** —

*[On line 2, Julia inserted "you" with a caret between "and" and "to", apparently to repair the dropped word at the end of line 1; the consolidated reading restores it in brackets at its grammatical home.]*
