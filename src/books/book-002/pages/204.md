A large half-tone newspaper photograph of a tablescape. An ornate cut-glass lamp with a floral-printed shade stands at the left of a drop-leaf table; to the right is a tall floral arrangement in a footed vase. Between them on the table top sit a small inlaid box, a folded paper or envelope, and a pair of dark eyeglasses.

Printed caption beneath the photograph:

> A lamp, a cigarette box and an arrangement of pansies, larkspur, snapdragon and shrimp plant on a drop-leaf occasional table make up this charming display. It was done by Mrs. J. P. Walker Jr.
