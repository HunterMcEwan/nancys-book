A worn cream envelope mounted on the page, flap open and partly torn at the upper edge.

Across the top of the flap, in brown ink:

> Four leaf clover sent
> to Baby at his birth
> by Amy.

At the lower right, in blue ink in a different hand:

> Little Sam
