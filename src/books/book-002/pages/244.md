notes: |
  Souvenir-style commemorative birth certificate from Orange Memorial Hospital, Orlando, Florida, certifying the birth of **Theodore McEwan Green** to Mr. and Mrs. Theodore R. Green at 8:37 P.M. on Saturday, 13 July 1968. Hollister 'Inscribed Birth Certificate' form, engraved in Gothic and copperplate type, with the hospital's embossed gold-paper seal at lower left. Three signatures: an attending physician (cursive — first name appears to be 'Thomas' with a middle initial 'L' or 'I', surname starting with 'S' — uncertain reading, possibly 'Thomas L. Slau' / 'Thomas I. Sears' / similar) and two hospital directors, **Garth Walker** and **James W. Quinn** (the long-serving administrator of Orange Memorial Hospital 1954–1977). The middle name 'McEwan' preserves the maternal family surname — the child's mother is Nancy Fletcher McEwan Green (Mrs. Theodore R. Green), Amy FitzSimons Walker's granddaughter and the namesake of 'Nancy's book.'

  Embossed gold paper seal of Orange Memorial Hospital at lower left. Lower-left fine print: "HOLLISTER Inscribed BIRTH CERTIFICATE / H4". James W. Quinn served as administrator/director of Orange Memorial Hospital from 1954 to 1977, so his signature here in 1968 is consistent with his tenure. Theodore McEwan Green is the son of Nancy Fletcher McEwan (b. 13 Sept 1946, Orlando) + Theodore Robert Green (b. 30 April 1938, Arcade NY) — they married 3 Feb 1968 in Orlando, so Theodore McEwan Green was born five months later. Nancy is the daughter of Mary Ann Walker McEwan + Lt. Oswald Beverley 'Bo' McEwan, and the granddaughter of the album's compiler Amy FitzSimons Walker.

>
> Orlando, Florida

> **Birth Certificate**

> **This Certifies** that **THEODORE McEWAN GREEN** was born to **MR. & MRS. THEODORE R. GREEN** in this Hospital at **8:37 P.M.** on **Saturday** the **Thirteenth** day of **July 19 68**

> **In Witness Whereof** the said Hospital has caused this Certificate to be signed by its duly authorized officer and its Official Seal to be hereunto affixed.

>
> /s/ *Garth Walker*, Director

> /s/ *James W. Quinn*, Director
