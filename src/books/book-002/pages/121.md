A narrow vertical newspaper clipping pasted at the top of the page.

> **SENIOR HIGH TO**
> **GIVE DIPLOMAS**
>
> **AT TONIGHT EXERCISES**
>
> **C. J. Goodwin to Make**
> **Address to Pupils**

> The Senior High School graduating exercises will be held tonight at the [illegible] auditorium. C. J. Goodwin will make the address to the graduating class. Diplomas will be awarded by [illegible].

[The article continues with the roster of graduates set in small print across the remainder of the column. The list is largely illegible at this resolution; Mary Ann Walker is presumed to appear among the names, as she graduated Savannah Senior High School in 1933 per the captions on the facing portraits.]
