A lined-paper album page with two studio photographs of the compiler as a young child.

**Right (large cabinet photograph):** A small girl, perhaps four or five years old, in a long white dress, seated on a tasseled velvet chair and holding a mandolin across her lap. The photographer's imprint at the lower edge of the mount reads:

> Quinby's Studio
> 26 King St.
> Charleston S.C.

**Left (smaller faded print):** A carte-de-visite-sized print, much faded and dog-eared, of a young girl in a light dress. The features are barely legible.

**Caption in blue ink to the right of the smaller print:**

> Amy Perry Fitz Simons
