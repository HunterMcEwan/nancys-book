A folded notecard, viewed from the back. At the centre is an embossed and ink-printed monogram in elegant cursive capitals:

> N M^c G

*[Personal monogram of Nancy McEwan Green: the central letter is rendered with a small underlined superscript 'c' in the 'Mc' style, marking the 'McEwan' of her middle name. The body of the letter — visible faintly in reverse through the paper — opens on the next page (268).]*
