A cream-colored card with the university seal embossed at the upper left and the legend **Duke University** embossed in blackletter at the lower edge. All elements are blind-stamped (no ink); the words read by raised relief.

> *[seal]* Universitas Dukiana
> Eruditio et Religio
>
> **Duke University**
