notes: "Phase 2 enhanced pass: the phase-1 'Licensed to Wed' list was largely fabricated from a misread of the small newsprint; this pass replaces it with the actual names visible on the clipping. The page (lined paper) carries two items: a Jacksonville newspaper clipping mounted at upper center and an engraved wedding invitation on cream card stock mounted at lower center. The clipping is the 'Licensed to Wed' column from THE SUNDAY TIMES-UNION, Jacksonville, Florida, Sunday, June 22, 1941. The entry 'James Pickens Walker Jr. and Ann Seymour Knight' is underlined in pencil on the clipping. The invitation is from Mr. and Mrs. Albion Williamson Knight for the marriage of their daughter Ann Seymour to Mr. James Pickens Walker Jr. at the Church of the Good Shepherd, Jacksonville, on Thursday, June 26 (1941). A pencilled '1941' is written at the lower left of the page."
