notes: "An album page (lined paper) carrying two items: a Jacksonville newspaper clipping mounted at the upper center, and an engraved wedding invitation on cream card stock mounted at center below it. The clipping is the 'Licensed to Wed' column from THE SUNDAY TIMES-UNION, Jacksonville, Florida, Sunday, June 22, 1941, listing marriage-license applicants for the week. The invitation is from Mr. and Mrs. Albion Williamson Knight for the marriage of their daughter Ann Seymour to Mr. James Pickens Walker Jr. at the Church of the Good Shepherd, Jacksonville, on Thursday, June 26 (1941). A pencilled '1941' is written at the lower left of the page."
