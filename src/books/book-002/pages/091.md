In black ink on cream paper:

> Wednesday.
>
> July 14th.
>
> Dear little Daughter,
>
> I have been so troubled over your long silence knowing you have been working beyond your strength, and that you are worn out mentally and physically. I am just sending a line on this my [sister Susan]'s birthday to tell you how often I think of you and with gratitude to God
