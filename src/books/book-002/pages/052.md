Three photographs mounted on a lined album page, each captioned in ink.

**Upper photograph** — group of seven young men seated on grass, several in vests and white shirtsleeves, two wearing dark suit coats. Mounted in an embossed paper folder. Caption beneath:

> James Pickens Walker — B. & O. Engineers
> With B. & O. — 1903 – 1906.

**Lower left photograph** — small sepia print of a woman seated outdoors among shrubbery, holding a baby in a long white gown. Caption beneath:

> Mrs. S. C. Walker & Amy Walker.

**Lower right photograph** — small sepia print of a haystack with a figure standing beside it, and what appears to be a horse-drawn farm implement at the right. Caption beneath:

> Mrs. S. C. Walker & J. P. Walker.
