A single oval-mounted studio portrait at the center of the page.

The sitter is a young woman with dark hair piled in a high pompadour, wearing a pale shirtwaist with a soft ruffled or pleated bodice. She is shown in three-quarter view, her head turned to her right and one hand raised lightly to her cheek. The studio backdrop behind her is a softly clouded gray.
