**Upper — envelope.** A small cream envelope with a circular New York, N.Y. postmark dated APR 17 1953, 9:30 AM, with a wavy-line cancellation across a 3-cent Ohio Sesquicentennial commemorative stamp. Addressed in pencil in a slanting hand:

> Mr. J. P. Walker
> 303 Norman Rd. Greenacres
> Portsmouth
> Virginia

Below the address, partly obscured, a fainter line in the same pencil hand:

> Portsmouth [*illegible*]

**Lower — photograph.** A black-and-white snapshot, slightly trimmed at the corners, showing a small fair-haired boy in shorts and a striped shirt standing on a lawn in a backyard garden. He has his left leg in a cast and is supported on crutches under each arm. Behind him are flowering shrubs, a low brick wall or planter, and a tall tree at the right. Captioned beneath in blue ink:

> Picture Walter Corbell — Portsmouth 1953.
