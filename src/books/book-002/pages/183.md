> ### WILL BE MATRON OF HONOR IN PROMINENT WEDDING
>
> *[Head-and-shoulders studio portrait of a young woman wearing a pale draped neckline gown and a triple-strand pearl necklace; photographer's credit at lower right of image:]* Dittinger-Woodward
>
> Mrs. Marcus Latanna Corbett Jr. of Richmond, Va., will be the matron of honor at the wedding of her sister, Miss Mary Ann Walker, and Oswald Beverley McEwan, which will take place at 8 o'clock Wednesday evening, May 14, in the Church of the Good Shepherd.
