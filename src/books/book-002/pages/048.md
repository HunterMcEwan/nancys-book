A single large pasted photograph mounted across the page, oriented sideways relative to the album.

In its proper orientation the print shows a two-story plantation house with a long pillared front porch and a roofline broken by multiple dormer windows. A separate small outbuilding or wing stands at the left; tall trees rise behind the house to the right, and an open foreground of grass slopes down toward the camera. The image is sepia-toned and slightly faded.

**Caption (handwritten in brown ink along the right margin of the album page):**

> Mount Hope.
