notes: "Fourth page of the letter. Dee continues her reflections on the August 1940 flooding around Richmond: because there's no water near her own home she doesn't feel the effect of all the trouble, but it has rained all week and she half expects mildew to break out on her. She hopes the weather will clear in a day or two. The page closes with the start of a new sentence, 'Wish we could look forward to'."
