Closing thoughts on missing her family and an invitation to visit, then a domestic vignette: she and her husband have been playing casino in the evenings, the housekeeping has been easy, and a gift of ham and avocados from her mother (or stuffed peppers made with the ham) was a 'grand help.' Written in graphite pencil on the same notepaper. The page breaks mid-sentence: 'in my straightening up'."

> would be so nice if I could see you-all more often if you were nearer to me. Come again soon — you know you're always welcome —
>
> Not much news 'cause we've been so lazy — just playing casino at nights. Grand to state, the house-keeping so far has been mighty easy. The ham and avocados were a grand help! I'm sorry to say they're gone now — even to stuffing peppers with the ham!
>
> I'm furious with myself 'cause in my straightening up
