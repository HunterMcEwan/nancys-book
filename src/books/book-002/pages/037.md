A single mounted cabinet photograph of a boy, perhaps nine or ten years old, in a dark double-breasted jacket with a watch chain looped across the front. Head-and-shoulders, three-quarter view. The photographer's imprint at the lower edge of the mount is partly illegible — apparently a Washington, D.C., studio.

**Caption in blue ink beneath the photograph:**

> **James Pickens Walker** — **1896 — 1897 — 1898**
> [graded?] school in Washington D.C. —
> **Peabody School** — 1897 — 1898 — 1899

*[The dates of attendance, and the underlining of the school name, are in the compiler's hand. The third date in each line is faint.]*
