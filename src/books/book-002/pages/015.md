because our family Mr. While we were on the plantation Dr. Grimbal took care of us and later on Dr. Taylor.

Uncle Hit made regular trips down. We loved him. Full of fun — and he always brought us presents. He came to go over [investrass?] affairs with Had. I never remember Aunt Sammy coming.

John and Louisa came with Aunt Helen Porcher. The first time they came to the country, John saw a cow he asked: "Does it walk through the house?"

Mauma Mim's life was a very full one. The "mistress" on a plantation never knew what what she might be called on to do — from intertaining artists and authors, and notables — to being judge, and jury, in plantation arguments. As — when Thomas Berry — who didn't much [enright?] — married a girl and then locked up his nation and refused to fish her.
