A typewritten sheet, the second of a two-page document continuing from page 33.

> **Elizabeth** daughter of **Nancy**, born 27th April 1841.
>
> ⋯⋯⋯⋯
>
> **January** son of **Selina** born 11th March 1843.
>
> ⋯⋯⋯⋯
>
> **May** son of **Selina** born 28th February 1845.
>
> ⋯⋯⋯⋯
>
> **John** the child of **Adam** and **Pleasant**, born at **Goose Creek** on 14th Feb. 1852.

> (My great-grandfather's second wife was a widow, **Maria Ann Mauger** (pronounced **Major**). She had been a Hedley and these notes were made by my mother from the old Hedley Bible. After Mr. **Edward Perry** died his widow married for the 3rd time **Col. Thomas Fuller** from **Beaufort**.)

> (**Amy F. Walker**)

*[The lower half of the sheet is blank. The signature reads **Amy F. Walker** — the typewritten letter shows the unmistakable double horizontal cross-bars of an F.]*
