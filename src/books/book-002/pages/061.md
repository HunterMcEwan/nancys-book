Above and around an empty mount at upper left:

> This is a picture of Harry taken about that time.
>
> We went to W. Va. and stayed on the farm — That was the year Mr. Walker bought the house in Philippi.

Beneath the photograph of the living-room interior:

> This is a picture of our first living room — on the farm in W. Va.

Continuing below, in blue ink (with a small inserted date "1909" above "March"):

> We stayed there until March 1909 when Puck was called back to the A. C. L. We stayed in Florence at the hotel there until summer, and in the fall we went to Marion N. C. I was pregnant — and I stayed there until *[struck through: "then I went"]*

In a slightly different hand along the bottom edge:

> to/the plantation [stayed?] until March 6th when I went to Char. to the Roper [Sanitorium]. Say nice [people the] [people].
