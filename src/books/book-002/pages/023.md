> went into the cotton field and helped the people pick cotton. We went to the dog and pony show on Tuesday and we are going to the circus on ~~Friday~~ Friday. I am reading a very nice book now called the Scottish Chiefs. Give my love to Miss Kate and Hettie.
>
> Your loveing little friend
> Amy P. Fitz Simons
