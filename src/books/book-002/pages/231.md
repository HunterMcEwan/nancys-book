*[Printed at the top of the sheet in large red block capitals:]*

> **NANCY McEWAN**

Dear Grandmother,

We have been making valentines. We are very well. Chris and I got good reportcards. I got S and good remarks.

Chris got all A's and B's.

I want you to come down soon.

Love, Nancy
