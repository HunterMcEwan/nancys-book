notes: "Fifth page of Julia's letter of Feb. 10, 1945. Julia describes how Mr. MacKay and several other colleagues at the Southern Railway are 'taking me in hand' and helping with practical matters she could never have tackled alone. She remarks that it is a beautiful tribute to 'Pick' (as the men called Coburn) that his friends are so eager to help her. The page closes addressing Puck directly: Mr. MacKay had told her, looking really distressed about it, that Coburn was to have received a promotion. The sentence continues on page 153."
