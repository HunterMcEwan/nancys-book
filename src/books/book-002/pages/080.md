A short message in pencil, in a flowing adult hand, recording a child's words:

> "Tell Mama I am
> Mr McGregor, & I
> am missin' her —
> Dilly"

*[The quotation marks suggest the message is a transcribed dictation of the child's own words. "Dilly" is likely a household nickname for one of the Walker children. The reference is to Mr. McGregor, the gardener in Beatrix Potter's *The Tale of Peter Rabbit* (1902).]*
