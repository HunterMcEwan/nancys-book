*[The right-margin list is written in pencil, sideways, and very faint against the page. The first name reads clearly as "Ilee" - the family nickname for Emma Dee Walker Corbell, the groom's elder sister (b. 18 Oct 1915; see p102). The second is "Mary Ann" - Mary Ann Walker, the groom's younger sister (m. Lt. O. B. McEwan one month earlier on 14 May 1941; see pp187 - 188). The remaining four (Carol, Katie, Anne, Maxine) appear to be friends of the bride. The seventh name has resisted clean transcription: the final word reads "Lilly" or "Libby"; the word immediately preceding it could be either "great aunt" (a kinship modifier) or a surname like "Goldsmith". Phase-1's reading was "Sis - Mary Ann - Carol - Helen - Anne - Maxine - great aunt Lilly"; the first name is corrected to "Ilee" and the fourth from "Helen" to "Katie".]*
