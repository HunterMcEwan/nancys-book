A tall black-and-white photograph of an infant propped upright in a baby carriage, wearing a long-sleeved white gown. He has wisps of dark hair, dark eyes, and an alert open-mouthed expression turned slightly to one side. The hood of the pram and trees rise behind him; the chrome handle bar of the carriage in the foreground reflects a small dark figure -- presumably the photographer.

Pencil caption beneath the print, in two lines:

> Robert Lawrence Corbell 3rd -- Born Jan.
> 1940 -- St. Lukes Hospital -- Richmond. Va.

*[Subject is Robert Lawrence Corbell III (~1940), the eldest of the Corbell grandchildren in the family record. The caption's final abbreviation, previously read as illegible, is "Va." -- confirming Richmond, Virginia, as the place of birth. Phase-1's reading of the surname as "Cantwell" was a transcription error; the surname is Corbell.]*
