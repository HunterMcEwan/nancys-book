A tall black-and-white photograph of an infant propped upright in a baby carriage, wearing a long-sleeved white gown. He has wisps of dark hair, dark eyes, and an alert open-mouthed expression turned slightly to one side. The hood of the pram and trees rise behind him; the chrome handle bar of the carriage in the foreground reflects a small dark figure — presumably the photographer.

> Robert Lawrence [Cantwell] 3rd — Born Jan.
