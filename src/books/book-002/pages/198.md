notes: 'Album page with two stamped envelopes mounted one above the other on otherwise blank ruled paper. Each bears a 3-cent purple Statue of Liberty stamp and a Portsmouth postmark; both are addressed to Mrs. J. P. Walker in Jacksonville. The upper envelope is addressed in childish block lettering in pencil; the lower in a clearer adult hand in dark ink. A pencilled note along the left edge identifies the upper as Robin''s first letter.'
