**Calling card (upper left):**

A small printed card in copperplate. The engraved portion at upper right reads:

> St. Cecilia Society
>
> 1915

Filled in below, by hand in dark ink:

> Admit Mr. + Mrs. J. P. Walker

*[Reading of "St. Cecilia" is uncertain — the engraved letters are faint; the Walkers' membership in the St. Cecilia Society in Charleston would be consistent with their social circle.]*

**Tipped-in sheet (right):**

A folded sheet of cream paper, hinged by its left edge to the album page and lying verso-up, so the writing on the reverse shows through in mirror image. The recto continues onto pages 84 and 85 as Mannie's bequest list. At the upper right corner of the slip, in pencil:

> List of things given me from the old home —
