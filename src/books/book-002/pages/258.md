A blank sheet of personal stationery with the monogram **NMcG** printed in dark blue script script at the center. Faint pencilled handwriting bleeds through from the opposite face of the paper but is not legible from this side.

*[The monogram "NMcG" is the writer of the letter on pages 259–260: "Nancy McGuire" (presumably) — signed "Nancy" at the close on page 259. The show-through visible here is the verso of that letter.]*
