> **Women's News** — *Orlando Sentinel*, Classified GA 3-8511 — Sunday, May 29, 1966 — 3—E
>
> ## Surprises Are All Silver
>
> By **DEBBIE ORLANDO**
>
> When Bo McEwan planned a surprise 25th wedding anniversary party for Mary Ann recently, Agnes (their maid) was in cohoots [*sic*] and each detail of the buffet supper was perfect. The Jerry Rosses invited the couple over to their house before going out to dinner. When Bo insisted on going home for a minute there were no cars in evidence to indicate that a few close friends and relatives were on hand, plus members of the wedding party from other parts of Florida.
>
> Bibba and Ray Richardson, Ann Wright and Judson Freeman, Sara Lee and Bill Scheu, Ed and Dottie Gay and Ann and Peck Walker [*… text continues; the next item runs:*] Margaret Watkins for a spring dance weekend at St. Anne's in Charlottesville, Va. Taking advantage of a free Saturday morning, Jim phoned his brother, Chris, who is in law school at Washington and Lee, in Lexington, Va.
>
> Chris told Jim to hop on a bus and meet him in Staunton for lunch which he thought he did. After an interminable period of riding, Jim asked his seat companion where they were headed and found that it was in the opposite direction from Staunton, towards Richmond.
>
> He pulled the cord, dismounted in the middle of nowhere and caught a ride back to Charlottesville with a former Hill student. Chris must have wondered what happened to his younger brother, who arrived back in time for the dance and otherwise delightful weekend.

*[Photo captions, beneath two head-and-shoulders portraits:]*

> **Mary Ann McEwan**     **Claudia Stainton**
