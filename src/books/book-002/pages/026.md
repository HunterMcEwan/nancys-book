**Top photograph** (interior view of a Victorian parlor):

> Living room at Mount Hope.

**Left and lower-left photographs** (elderly woman, seen at full length under live oaks hung with Spanish moss); caption beneath the middle photograph:

> "Mam'mie".
> (Jennie Perry FitzSimons)
> as she was in my early girlhood —

**Lower right** (an oval vignette portrait of a young girl with shoulder-length hair); caption beneath:

> A. P. F. S.

*[The initials are Amy Perry FitzSimons — the compiler herself as a child.]*
