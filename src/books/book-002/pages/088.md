notes: "Album page with seven sepia snapshots clustered loosely in three columns on a ruled sheet, with handwritten captions in blue ink between them. Top right corner inscribed 'Jacksonville Fla.' establishing the location for the whole page. The long blue-ink caption reads 'Jimmey Miller' (the y is unambiguous; 'Jim—' not 'Jer—'). The brace in the caption groups the upper three children (Pickens, Ilee, Mary Ann) separately from the beach group (Ilee, Doris, Jimmey Miller). The 'Pickens' here is the compiler's son James Pickens Walker Jr. (Bo), age ~10–16 in the early-Jacksonville period."
