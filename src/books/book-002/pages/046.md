A single large pasted photograph mounted across the page, oriented sideways relative to the album.

In its proper orientation the print shows a varsity football team posed for a portrait — roughly a dozen and a half young men in striped jerseys and padded football trousers, arranged in two or three tiers, with a manager or coach in dark coat at the right edge. The image is faded and slightly overexposed.

**Caption (handwritten in pencil along the right-hand margin of the album page):**

> Jamie — Picture taken Washington & Lee University — Fall back varsity team 1901
>
> 1901 – 1903

*["Jamie" is presumably James, a member of the team and the subject the page is preserving. Washington and Lee University is at Lexington, Virginia.]*
