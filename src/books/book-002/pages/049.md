Two photographs mounted vertically on a lined album page.

**Upper photograph** — oval head-and-shoulders portrait of three young men in dark suits, white collars, and ties. Captioned in ink beneath:

> J. W. Warner — J. P. Walker — W. J. Pendleton —
> Washington & Lee — 1902.

**Lower photograph** — oval three-quarter-length portrait of a young woman wearing a wide-brimmed hat trimmed with flowers or feathers, a dark bow at her neck, and a striped or patterned bodice. Captioned in ink beneath:

> Amy Perry Fitz Simons
> St. Mary's 1908.
