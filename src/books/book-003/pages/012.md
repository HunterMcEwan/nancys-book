notes: "First page of a multi-page adult letter in blue fountain-pen cursive on ruled letter paper, dated only 'Tuesday'. Signed 'Dee' on the continuation (scans 013/014). Addressed 'My Folks' — i.e., to her parents, who in the context of this archive are Mr. and Mrs. James Pickens Walker (Amy FitzSimons, the compiler, and her husband). The letter is largely domestic news about a small child, 'Puck,' who has been ill and is asking for 'one of Gam's drinks.' 'Gam' is the family nickname for Amy FitzSimons herself. 'Tressie' appears to be a sister or close relation of the writer. The matched continuation pages are scans 013, 014, and 016."

> Tuesday —
>
> My Folks —
>
> I'm so smart to remember to buy writing paper — smug about the whole thing — 'til I go to write a letter and find ruled paper! But as it is all in the family and only a line anyway, here goes.
>
> We're rocking along — not much change. The only thing that worries me is the thing Puck asks for constantly is "one of Gam's drinks". ~~Days~~ I worked and none suited. So after due deliberation and back tracking, I now pour ginger ale in a bowl,
